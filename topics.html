话题与旅程 · Topics & Journeys Topics & Journeys

本项目是一场从“被期待的人”到“自我觉醒的存在”的旅程。
以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架，我们一起走过：经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造的路径， 重建与自我、他人与时间的关系。

This project is a journey from “the expected one” to a self-awakened being.
With psychology as method and philosophy as backbone, we walk a pathway of experience → awareness → action → self-creation, rebuilding how we relate to ourselves, others, and time.

经验 · Experience Experience

从日常片段、情绪波动、关系中的拉扯开始——不急着解释，只是如实地看见。

We begin with everyday fragments, emotional tides, and relational tensions — not rushing to explain, simply learning to see what is there.

意识 · Awareness Awareness

在心理学与哲学的对话中，为这些经验找到语言：我在保护什么？我害怕什么？我真正渴望什么？

Through psychological and philosophical reflection, we find words for these experiences: What am I protecting? What am I afraid of? What do I truly long for?

行动 · Action Action

把洞见变成一小步可行的改变：一次对话、一条边界、一项新的尝试。

Turning insight into small, doable shifts: a conversation, a boundary, a new experiment in living.

自我创造 · Self-creation Self-creation

逐渐从“被期待”转向“自我选择”：用自己的语言讲述人生故事，成为自己生活的作者。

Gradually moving from living up to expectations toward making self-chosen commitments, becoming the author of your own story.

活动形式 · Formats Formats

  • 一对一心理咨询 / 对话
  • 小组工作坊：写作、绘画、对话、共读
  • 主题课程：关于自主性、关系、时间的深度探索
  • One-to-one counselling and conversations
  • Small-group workshops: writing, art, dialogue, shared reading
  • Thematic courses on autonomy, relationships, and time

适合谁 · Who This Is For Who This Is For

  • 正在经历学业、工作或城市变动的人
  • 在家庭期待与自我声音之间左右拉扯的人
  • 在亲密关系中感到困惑、反复重复某种模式的人
  • 对“我是谁”“我要往哪里去”有强烈疑问的人
  • Those navigating transitions in study, work, or place
  • Those caught between family expectations and their own voice
  • Those noticing repeated patterns in relationships
  • Those holding strong questions like “Who am I?” and “Where am I going?”
