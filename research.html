研究与发表 · Research & Publications Research & Publications

我的研究围绕“人在关系中如何成为自己”这一核心问题：在家庭、同伴与学校之间，在文化与迁移的流动中， 青少年与青年如何发展自主性？如何在压力、期待与爱之间，为自己找到一条路？

My research circles around one central question: how do people become themselves in relationships? Across family, peers, and school — and across cultures and transitions — how do adolescents and young adults develop autonomy and a sense of self amid pressure, expectations, and care?
