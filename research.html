My publications form three connected threads: motivation in school, autonomy in family and peer relationships, and compassion and mental health in minority groups.

THEME 01 · SCHOOL & MOTIVATION THEME 01 · SCHOOL & MOTIVATION
学校转衔中的学习动机
Learning motivation across school transitions

从「成绩好不好」转向「孩子为什么学，以及在什么环境下愿意学」。

Moving from “how well do students perform” to “why do they learn – and in which environments do they want to?”.

  • 追踪动机、压力与学校联结的变化轨迹。
  • 把结果转成学校与家庭可操作的调整方向。
  • Track trajectories of motivation, stress, and school relatedness.
  • Translate findings into concrete levers for schools and families.
THEME 02 · FAMILY & PEERS THEME 02 · FAMILY & PEERS
在亲子与同伴关系中长出的自主
Autonomy growing within parenting and friendships

用数据回答一个现实问题：
「怎么爱一个青少年，才既不失控，也不失去他/她？」

Answering a very practical question with data:
“How do we love teenagers without over-controlling – or losing them?”

  • 检验父母自主支持、心理控制、朋友支持与自主的双向关联。
  • 帮助父母在「界限」与「信任」之间找到平衡。
  • Study bidirectional links between parenting, friend support, and autonomy.
  • Offer evidence-based ways to balance boundaries and trust.

设计与关键洞见 Design & key insights
◎ 多层时间视角：日记与半年追踪结合，区分「今天特别不一样」和「长期走向」。 Multi-time scales: daily diary plus 6-month follow-ups, separating “today is different” from long-term trends.
↔ 双向影响：父母温暖与自主在每天互相推着前进；自主的提升，预示着半年后感知到的心理控制下降。 Bidirectional processes: warmth and autonomy reinforce each other daily; higher autonomy predicts later drops in perceived psychological control.
✦ 学校联结的延迟效应：转衔后感觉「学校是自己的地方」，半年后内在学业动机更高。 Lagged school effects: feeling the school “belongs to me” after transition predicts higher intrinsic motivation months later.
⇢ 实践指向：聚焦可调整的日常互动（多一点温暖、多听一次意见），而非把责任都压在孩子身上。 Practice-facing: highlights small, malleable interactions (a bit more warmth, one more chance to choose) instead of blaming adolescents.
PREFACE · 序言 PREFACE · BEGINNING
从「要懂事」到「我也可以为自己而活」
From “be good” to “I may live for myself”

这本论文的起点是一个熟悉的场景：
被反复要求「要懂事、要争气」的孩子，还能为自己留多少空间？

The thesis began with a familiar scene:
when a child keeps hearing “be good, do well”, how much room remains for their own life?

  • 把「叛逆」看成在为自主与尊严辩护。
  • 把「不想学」看成动机被压力压扁的信号。
  • Re-reads “rebellion” as a defence of autonomy and dignity.
  • Re-reads “no motivation” as a sign of pressure crushing curiosity.
JOURNEY · 过程 JOURNEY · PROCESS
一边做研究，一边学会不再只怪自己
Doing research while learning not to blame myself alone

论文不是一条直线，而是一段反复重来、重写、重组的路。
在数据与模型里，我也学会：原来很多痛苦，是系统不够友善，而不是我不够努力。

The thesis was not a straight line but a series of returns, rewrites, and rebuilds.
Through the data, I learned: much of my struggle reflected harsh systems, not a lack of effort.

  • 温暖的一点点增加，会在次日变成更大的自主。
  • 学校联结可以在半年后托住孩子的学习意愿。
  • Small increases in warmth predicted more autonomy the next day.
  • School connectedness supported motivation months later.
