研究发表 · 从故事到证据 Research · Where stories meet evidence

在这里，你会看到我这些年围绕自主性、人际关系、青少年与心理健康的研究足迹： 从家庭、同伴到学校与线上空间，从横断面到密集纵向追踪， 每一项工作，都是在回答同一个问题—— 人如何在复杂世界里，仍然活得像自己？

Here you’ll find my research around autonomy, relationships, adolescence, and mental health: from family and peers to school and digital spaces, from cross-sectional designs to intensive longitudinal work. Each study circles the same core question: How do we stay ourselves in a complicated world?

这页不是简历，而是一张地图：把论文与发表，放回它们真正关心的人—— 也就是像你一样，在成长、关系与选择中摸索的人身上。

This page is less a CV and more a map: placing publications back into the lives they care about—people like you, navigating growth, relationships, and choice.

内在之岛的成长：在家庭、同伴与学校之间发展自主性的青少年
Growing an Inner Isle: Adolescent Autonomy Across Family, Peers, and School
一项多波段、发展视角的追踪研究
A multi-wave, developmental study on autonomy and adjustment
发展心理学博士论文 · Utrecht University · 年份（请在此替换）
PhD Dissertation in Developmental Psychology · Utrecht University · Year (to be updated)
  • 从「被期待的孩子」到「可以自己做决定的青年」：追踪自主性在家庭、同伴与学校中的发展。
  • 结合纵向问卷与密集日记数据，考察日常情绪、冲突与支持如何影响长期的心理健康。
  • 提出一个整合模型：在温暖、结构与自主支持并存的环境中，青少年更有可能发展出稳定而柔韧的自我感。
  • Traces how adolescents move from “expected child” to “self-directed young person” across family, peers, and school.
  • Combines longitudinal surveys with intensive diary data to link daily emotion, conflict, and support to long-term mental health.
  • Proposes an integrated model: when warmth, structure, and autonomy support co-exist, adolescents are more likely to develop a steady yet flexible sense of self.
