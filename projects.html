研究项目与教学/指导 Research projects & supervision

每一个项目，都是一群人把各自的生命经验、疑问与好奇，暂时编织在一起。 Every project is a temporary weaving of people’s experiences, questions, and curiosities.

参与的研究项目 Research projects

InTransition Project

聚焦青少年在重要转折期（如升学、迁移、家庭变动）中的适应过程，
通过密集纵向数据与多方法设计，追踪情绪、关系与自我感受的变化。 Focuses on adolescent adjustment during key transitions (e.g., school changes, relocation, family shifts), using intensive longitudinal designs to track emotion, relationships, and sense of self over time.

IMAGINE Project

关注青少年与青年人在想象未来、自我定位与关系选择时的心理过程，
探索“我将成为什么样的人？”这一问题背后的希望、恐惧与文化脚本。 Explores how adolescents and young adults imagine their future, position themselves, and make relational choices, unpacking the hopes, fears, and cultural scripts behind “Who am I becoming?”.

教学与学生指导 Teaching & supervision

· 指导 12 名本科生，完成关于自主性发展、人际关系、青少年心理健康等主题的毕业项目。
· 指导 2 名硕士实习/研究生项目，聚焦心理健康教育与发展心理学应用。

对我来说，“指导”从来不是单向传授，而是与学生一起：
· 把模糊的兴趣，变成可以被研究的问题；
· 把枯燥的统计，变成可以读懂生命故事的工具；
· 把毕业论文，变成他们人生中第一份真正“属于自己”的作品。 · Supervised 12 bachelor theses on autonomy, relationships, and youth mental health.
· Supervised 2 master students in mental health education and developmental applications.

For me, supervision is not one-way teaching but a shared process:
· Turning vague interests into researchable questions;
· Turning statistics into tools for reading life stories;
· Turning a thesis into a first piece of work that truly “belongs” to the student.
