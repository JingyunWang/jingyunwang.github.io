共创项目 · 把研究落在真实的土壤里 Co-created projects · bringing research into real lives

我的工作，一直站在三条线的交汇处：
发展心理学与动机研究 · 咨询与团体实践 · 教学与系统设计。
共创项目，就是让这三条线，在你的学校、机构、团队或产品里交织成一条可行的路径。

My work sits at the intersection of developmental & motivation research · counselling and group work · teaching and system design . Co-created projects weave these strands into something concrete in your school, organisation, team, or product.

我关心的，不只是「项目好看」，而是：
这个方案是不是真正支持自主性、人际关系、学习动机与身心健康， 能不能在你们现有的现实中活下去。

What matters is not a “pretty” project but whether it genuinely supports autonomy, relationships, learning motivation, and mental health – and survives in your real-world constraints.

下方你会看到：适合和我共创的伙伴类型、常见项目形态、一些项目片段， 以及如果你想合作，我们可以如何开始。

Below you’ll find: types of partners I work best with, common project formats, some project snapshots, and how we could start something together.

If your work touches learning motivation, parenting and relationships, autonomy, social support, AI & mental health, self-exploration, or meaning-making , there is likely a good overlap between us.

我适合和谁一起共创？ Who I’m a good collaborator for

简单来说：你既关心效果，也在乎方法是否合乎人性； 想把理论和数据用好，但不想让一线的学生、老师、来访者或员工被新的「形式主义」压垮。

In short, you care about impact and about humane methods. You want to use theory and data well without overburdening students, staff, or clients with another layer of box-ticking.

教育系统 · 中学 / 大学 Education · schools & universities

在校内做一些真正被学生需要的事

Doing what students actually need on campus

适合：教研组、学生工作部门、心理中心、过渡年级项目团队（如初中/高中/大学过渡）。

For teaching teams, student support, counselling centres, and transition programmes (e.g. primary–secondary, secondary–university).

  • 共建：学习动机与自主性提升项目。
  • 共建：亲师生三方对话/家长工作坊。
  • 共建：适应过渡期的团体课程或日记/追踪研究。
  • Co-designing motivation and autonomy programmes.
  • Parent–teacher–student dialogues and workshops.
  • Transition-focused groups or diary/longitudinal studies.
心理服务 · 青年支持机构 Counselling & youth services

让支持更系统、更可持续

Making support more systematic and sustainable

适合：心理咨询中心、热线/在线支持、青年服务与公益机构。

For counselling centres, helplines/online services, youth and community organisations.

  • 共建：以自主性和关系为核心的团体/课程架构。
  • 共建：服务评估框架与追踪工具（问卷、日记、访谈）。
  • 共建：对一线助人者的督导与工作坊。
  • Group curricula centred on autonomy and relatedness.
  • Service evaluation frameworks and follow-up tools.
  • Workshops and supervision models for frontline helpers.
企业 / 组织发展团队 Organisations & teams

在效率与人之间，找到不自欺的平衡

Finding a non-self-deceptive balance between performance and people

适合：关心员工心理健康、学习文化与长期发展，而不仅是短期 KPI 的团队。

For teams who care about mental health, learning culture, and sustainable growth – not just KPIs.

  • 共建：以自主性支持为核心的管理与反馈训练。
  • 共建：高压力岗位的心理健康与复原力项目。
  • 共建：员工发展对话工具（而不是形式主义打分表）。
  • Autonomy-supportive leadership and feedback training.
  • Mental health and resilience projects in high-pressure roles.
  • Development conversations tools, not just rating forms.
数字产品 / AI 心理健康 Digital & AI mental health

不只是「有一个 App」，而是它真的有人用

Beyond “having an app”: building something people use

适合：想在产品中认真处理心理健康、动机、自我探索与社交支持的团队。

For teams integrating mental health, motivation, self-exploration, or social support into products.

  • 共建：基于动机与发展心理学的干预路径与内容架构。
  • 共建：用户旅程地图与伦理底线（不以「上瘾」为成功指标）。
  • 共建：实验设计、数据分析与科研合作发表。
  • Intervention flows based on motivation and developmental science.
  • User journey mapping and ethical boundaries (success ≠ addiction).
  • Study design, data analysis, and research collaboration.
几类常见的「共创通道」 Common channels of co-creation

不同的资源与需求，可以走不同的通道。你可以把下面三类当作 「选项卡」，看看哪一类跟你现在最贴近。

Depending on your context, we might lean more on one of these channels.

CHANNEL 01 · RESEARCH × PRACTICE CHANNEL 01 · RESEARCH × PRACTICE
研究合作通道：从问题，到设计，到数据与发表
Research collaboration: from questions to design and publications

适合：希望在真实场景里做纵向/日记研究、干预实验的团队， 并希望结果能反馈到实践与政策中。

For teams wanting to run longitudinal/diary studies or interventions in real-world settings and feed findings back into practice and policy.

  • 一起打磨研究问题，使之贴近一线困惑（而不是只好看）。
  • 共建：研究设计、量表选择/编制、日记路径与伦理方案。
  • 数据分析（多水平、纵向模型、动机与关系变量）、论文与报告撰写。
  • Sharpening questions from frontline realities, not just theory.
  • Designing studies, measures, diaries, and ethical protocols.
  • Analysing data, and co-authoring papers and practice reports.
CHANNEL 02 · PROGRAMMES & TRAINING CHANNEL 02 · PROGRAMMES & TRAINING
课程 / 团体 / 工作坊通道：为真实人群设计可走完的路
Programmes & trainings: designing journeys people can finish

适合：希望有系统性课程/团体，而不是零散讲座的学校、机构与企业。

For partners wanting structured programmes or groups rather than one-off talks.

  • 共建：青少年/青年心力与学习动机课程（线下/线上混合）。
  • 共建：家长、公教人员、管理者的心理学与沟通训练。
  • 共建：围绕自主性、人际、生命意义的系列团体或读书会框架。
  • Youth-focused motivation and wellbeing courses (offline/online).
  • Training for parents, educators, or managers in psychology-informed communication.
  • Group and reading circle designs around autonomy, relationships, and meaning.
CHANNEL 03 · CONSULTING & DESIGN CHANNEL 03 · CONSULTING & DESIGN
咨询与产品设计通道：为系统和产品做心理「体检」
Consulting & design: a psychological “check-up” for systems and products

适合：已有现成项目或产品，希望从动机、自主性、人际与发展视角做升级，而不是推翻重来。

For existing programmes or products that need upgrading through motivation, autonomy, relational, and developmental lenses.

  • 项目/产品现状梳理：哪一部分在支持人，哪一部分在「耗人」。
  • 共建：更友好的用户旅程/学员路径，减少羞耻与「被管控感」。
  • 提出：循序渐进、现实可行的调整建议与评估指标。
  • Mapping what supports people and what quietly drains them.
  • Designing user journeys that reduce shame and control, and increase ownership.
  • Offering phased, realistic recommendations and evaluation markers.

很多合作会同时经过两三条通道： 例如，一边做教师训练，一边用日记法/问卷收集学生数据，最后形成对学校有帮助的报告或论文。

Many collaborations move across two or three channels: for example, combining teacher training with diary/questionnaire data and a final report or paper for the institution.

Keeping the human in screens and structures

In a world leaning into digital and AI solutions, I focus on protecting autonomy, connection, learning joy, and meaning while improving efficiency.

如果你有一个「想一起做做看」的念头 If you have a “what if we did this together” idea

合作，不需要一上来就很「完美」。
更现实的方式，是先用一个小而清晰的试点，来验证我们是否真的适合一起做事。

Collaboration doesn’t have to start perfectly. A small, well-defined pilot is usually the most honest test of fit.

STEP 01 STEP 01
写信告诉我：你具体在烦恼什么
Write briefly about your context & pain points

用几段话介绍你的团队/机构、服务对象、所在国家/地区，以及你现在最在意的 1–2 个问题。

A short description of your team, target group, country/region, and the 1–2 issues you care about most now.

STEP 02 STEP 02
线上沟通 · 对焦合作可能性
Online call · clarifying possibilities

我们一起判断：是否真的需要一个项目？还是先从一次讲座/内部讨论开始更合适。

We decide together whether you need a full project, or whether a talk, workshop, or consultation is a better first step.

STEP 03 STEP 03
共定一个小而清晰的试点
Co-define a small, clear pilot

确定一个时间有限、目标明确、可评估的小项目， 把资源集中在最有可能产生实际改变的地方。

We pick a pilot that is time-bounded, focused, and assessable, concentrating resources where change is actually possible.

STEP 04 STEP 04
实施 + 温和评估
Implementation + gentle evaluation

过程中，我们会用尽量不「打扰一线工作」的方式，收集少量但有意义的反馈与数据。

We gather light but meaningful feedback and data without overburdening frontline staff or participants.

STEP 05 STEP 05
一起判断：要不要放大、调整，或停在这里
Decide together: scale, adjust, or close with clarity

我们会坦诚评估：这个合作是否值得放大？如果不适合，也会好好收尾，而不是无限拖延。

We honestly assess whether to scale, adapt, or conclude the project – with a clear ending rather than endless extension.

如果你只是在「犹豫要不要做点什么」，也可以先给我发一封 不承诺任何合作的邮件， 我们一起把这份犹豫梳理清楚，再决定要不要继续往前。

If you’re just unsure whether to start anything at all, you’re welcome to send a zero-obligation email. We can clarify the hesitation first, then decide whether to move forward.

If you see your work reflected in these projects

You’re warmly invited to reach out with a rough idea, a fuzzy question, or a draft concept note. I’ll bring research, practice, and design together to help decide what would matter most for the real people you serve.

因为还有研究与教学工作，我通常会在约 10 天内回复。

Alongside research and teaching, I usually respond within about ten days.
