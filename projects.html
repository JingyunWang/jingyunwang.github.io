InTransition / Imagine（占位）

InTransition / Imagine (placeholder)

一个关于迁移、身份与心理适应的研究计划，关注人在“离开旧岸、尚未靠岸”时期的内在经验。

A research programme on migration, identity, and adaptation, focusing on what it means to live “between shores” — no longer where you were, not yet where you will be.

  • 方法：密集纵向数据、访谈与开放式书写
  • 主题：归属感、失重感、关系与时间感的变化
  • Methods: intensive longitudinal data, interviews, open-ended writing
  • Topics: belonging, weightlessness, relationships, and shifting sense of time

未来可能 · Emerging Ideas Emerging Ideas

我正在酝酿将自主性研究与实际心理支持结合的计划，例如：面向青少年和青年研究者的“内在岛屿”工作坊与写作计划。

I am developing projects that bring autonomy research into practice, such as “inner isle” workshops and writing circles for adolescents, young adults, and researchers.

学生指导 · Student Supervision Student Supervision

我曾指导 12 名本科生与 2 名硕士生，他们的课题从发展心理学、教育心理学到跨文化适应。

I have supervised 12 bachelor theses and 2 master theses, on topics ranging from developmental and educational psychology to cross-cultural adaptation.

  • 青少年自主性与父母支持方式
  • 同伴关系、排斥与心理健康
  • 在线学习环境中的动机与参与
  • 留学适应与身份认同
  • Adolescent autonomy and parental support
  • Peer relationships, exclusion, and mental health
  • Motivation and engagement in online learning
  • Study-abroad adjustment and identity
