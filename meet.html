与云相遇 · Meet Yun Meet Yun · The Person Behind Yunisle

我是王静云。一个发展心理学者、心理咨询师，也是一名长期在文化与语言之间漂流的旅人。
在寻屿，我邀请你，不再只做“被期待的人”，而是一点一点挖掘：当所有声音安静下来时，你真正是谁？

I am Jingyun Wang — a developmental psychologist, counsellor, and long-term wanderer between languages and cultures.
On the Yunisle, I invite you to move beyond being “who you should be” and gently uncover: who are you, when every outside voice grows quiet?

我的工作在科学与故事之间：一方面扎根于发展心理学和数据方法，另一方面深深相信，每一个人都在用自己的方式挣扎、守护、前进。

My work lives between science and stories: rooted in developmental psychology and intensive methods, yet always honoring how each person struggles, protects, and moves forward in their own way.

我如何陪伴 · How I Work How I Work With You

在咨询与对话中，我更像一位同行者：一起看见、一起整理，而不是给你标准答案。
我重视安全的边界、温度与诚实，尊重每个人独特的文化与生命语境。

In our conversations, I walk beside you — not to give you final answers, but to help you see, name, and hold what is already there. I value clear boundaries, warmth, and honesty, honoring your cultural and life context.

  • 以心理学研究为基础，但不用“标签”简单归类你
  • 在探索—领悟—行动之间来回，找到真正对你有用的一小步
  • 允许复杂、矛盾与模糊，不急着“想通”
  • Grounded in research, without reducing you to labels or checklists
  • Moving gently between exploration, insight, and action
  • Making room for complexity, ambivalence, and not-knowing

未来愿景 · Looking Ahead Future Vision

寻屿 Yunisle 对我来说，不只是一个网站，而是一座会慢慢长成的岛： 研究、写作、咨询、团体与课程都将在这里汇聚，成为一个让人可以“慢下来、听见自己、然后再出发”的空间。

For me, the Yunisle is not just a website, but an island that will keep growing: research, writing, counselling, groups, and courses will gradually gather here as a space where people can slow down, listen inward, and then step forward again.
