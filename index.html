Inner Isles of Becoming

Yunisle · An inner island
for those who are ready to become

Perhaps you’ve spent years being “the good one” — responsible, hardworking, meeting expectations — and still hear a quiet question at night: “Who am I, really? And who am I living for?”

Yunisle is an inner isle. A journey from who you’re expected to be to who you are becoming. Grounded in psychology, held by philosophy, we walk the path from experience → awareness → action → self-creation.

那些你没有说出口的问题

  • 总是照顾别人感受，可我自己的边界在哪里？
  • 在家庭、关系、工作里，我到底要满足谁的期待？
  • 我有很多“道理”，但为什么情绪还是反复困住我？
  • 我想改变，却总在关键一步退回原地。

如果你也在这些问题里看到自己，说明你已经在路上了—— 只是还缺一块柔软而坚定的“登陆之地”。

Questions you rarely say out loud

  • I'm always holding space for others — where are my own boundaries?
  • In family, work, relationships — whose expectations am I really serving?
  • I “know” a lot of psychology, so why do the same emotions still trap me?
  • I want to change, but I always step back at the last moment.

If you see yourself in these questions, you are already on the path — you might just need a gentler, steadier shore to land on.

一起来共建这座“内在之屿”

寻屿，不是我一个人的平台，而是一片可以被共同编织的岛屿：

  • 以研究为根：发展心理学、自我决定理论、关系与自主性的长期追踪研究。
  • 以真实经验为土壤：工作坊、团体、阅读会、日常生活的细小片段。
  • 以共创为方向：你可以是提问者、合作者、记录者，甚至是未来的引路人。

如果你愿意，我们可以一起设计话题、共建内容、发起项目， 让这座岛屿，真的长出属于我们的形状。

Co-creating an inner isle

Yunisle is not “my” platform alone. It is a shared island we shape together:

  • Rooted in research: developmental psychology, self-determination theory, and longitudinal work on autonomy and relationships.
  • Fed by lived experience: workshops, groups, reading circles, and everyday moments that quietly shift us.
  • Oriented toward co-creation: you may be a question-asker, a collaborator, a documenter, or even a future facilitator.

If you’re willing, we can design topics, build resources, and launch projects together — letting this isle grow into something that truly carries our names.
