Theory & Path · How we walk

Psychology as method, philosophy as spine, and your lived experience as soil.

Core lenses
Self-Determination Theory Attachment & Relationships Developmental & Narrative

We gently trace how needs for autonomy, competence, and relatedness have been met, misread, or silenced in your life.

“When we are no longer able to change a situation, we are challenged to change ourselves.” — Viktor Frankl

From experience to creation
  • Explore — name what has been lived but never fully spoken.
  • Insight — see patterns, meanings, and repeating scenes.
  • Action — try small, courageous experiments.
  • Self-creation — write, step by step, a life that feels like yours.

This is not about fixing you. This is about walking closer to who you already are, with clarity and kindness.

理论与路径 · 我们如何同行

以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架，以你的生命经验为土壤。

核心视角
自我决定理论（SDT） 依恋与关系 发展 · 叙事视角

我们会一起回看：你对自主、胜任、联结的需要， 曾如何被满足、被误解，或被压抑在生命的不同阶段。

「当一个人无法再改变环境时，他被迫面对的是： 他是否愿意改变自己。」—— 维克多·弗兰克

从经验到自我创造
  • 探索：为那些「经历过但从未好好说过」的东西命名。
  • 领悟：看见反复上演的模式、意义与情节。
  • 行动：尝试小而真实的行动实验。
  • 自我创造：一点点书写、实践一条更像自己的生活路径。

这里不是「修好你」，而是让你更接近那个一直在那里的自己， 用更清晰也更温柔的方式看待Ta。

Meet Yun · Behind the isle

Researcher, listener, facilitator of inner journeys.

I am Jingyun Wang, a developmental psychologist trained in China and the Netherlands, working with themes of autonomy, relationships, youth mental health, and intensive longitudinal data.

For over a decade I have been holding spaces: counselling, groups, workshops, reading circles, and quiet one-to-one conversations where people slowly come home to themselves.

My work bridges rigorous research and deeply humane presence.

Past & ongoing

  • PhD in Developmental Psychology, Utrecht University
  • Practice in counselling & psychoeducation (11+ years)
  • Research on autonomy, peer and family relationships, ILD
  • Groups & workshops on self-exploration and relationships

Vision

To build gentle, rigorous, and honest spaces where people can shift from “being the expected one” to becoming an awake, self-authored presence in their own lives.

Journeys & Topics

Spaces and conversations for those moving from “the expected one” to an awake, self-authored life.

  • From Expectation to Awakening — a guided journey into how you became “the good child / student / partner”, and what your own voice longs to say now.

These offerings are in development. If you feel a spark reading this, you are already part of the island.
