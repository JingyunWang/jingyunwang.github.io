一座为「正在成为的人」准备的岛屿 An isle for those in the midst of becoming

在云端与岛屿之间，
遇见正在成为的自己。 Between clouds and quiet isles,
you meet the self that is still becoming.

寻屿，不是把你「修好」，
而是陪你从他人期待中缓缓退场，
在心理学与哲学的对话里，重新与自己、与他人、与时间相遇。 Yunisle is not here to “fix” you.
It is a place to gently step out of others’ expectations,
and to meet yourself, others, and time anew through a quiet dialogue between psychology and philosophy.

也许，你正在经历—— Perhaps you find yourself in these moments—

  • 总是活在「应该」里，很少问过「我真正想要什么」。 Living inside “shoulds”, rarely asking “What do I truly want?”.
  • 在亲密关系、学业或工作中，用力满足别人，却渐渐失去自己的声音。 Working hard to meet others’ needs in love, study, or work, while losing your own voice.
  • 明知道哪里「不对劲」，却卡在原地，难以下决心行动。 Sensing that something is “off”, yet feeling stuck when it comes to real change.
  • 理性上懂了很多道理，但情绪仍然反复，难以安放。 Understanding many concepts rationally, while your emotions keep circling back.
走进这场旅程 Enter this journey 遇见云 Meet Yun

这不是「我带你走」，而是「我们一起走」。 This is not “I lead, you follow”, but “we walk together”.

寻屿是一场共建：
你带来真实的经验与问题，
我带来心理学与研究工具，
我们一起探索、领悟、行动，
共同创造一套属于你的生活语言。 Yunisle is a co-created space:
you bring your lived experience and questions,
I bring tools from psychology and research,
and together we explore, understand, act,
and slowly craft a language of life that is uniquely yours.

一起共建 · A Co-Created Space Co-Creating This Isle

这里欢迎你：以参与者、合作者、共创者的身份——
无论你是来体验成长活动、提出研究问题、
一起设计新的课程与实验，
或是单纯想找一个可以「慢下来」的地方。 Here you are invited in as participant, collaborator, and co-creator—
whether you join a journey group, bring in a research question,
co-design new courses or studies,
or simply look for a gentler pace to breathe and reflect.

这场旅程适合—— This journey is for—

  • 在亲密关系、家庭或学业工作中，感到被期待压得喘不过气的人。 Those weighed down by expectations in relationships, family, or work.
  • 想要更理解自己、理解他人，却不想被快餐化建议「安慰」的人。 Those who long to understand self and others, beyond quick advice and slogans.
  • 愿意在探索—领悟—行动之间来回往返，慢慢练习「成为自己」的人。 Those willing to move again and again between exploration, insight, and action.

我们用什么在同行？ What do we walk with?

  • 发展心理学、动机理论与自我决定理论。 Developmental psychology, motivation research, and self-determination theory.
  • 多流派心理咨询的实践经验与团体带领。 Experience across therapeutic approaches and group facilitation.
  • 对哲学、文学与日常生活的温柔好奇。 A gentle curiosity toward philosophy, literature, and everyday life.

你可以如何加入共建？ How can you co-create?

  • 参与主题旅程或工作坊。 Join themed journeys or workshops.
  • 提出研究／实践问题，一起设计研究或团体。 Bring research/practice questions to co-design studies or groups.
  • 作为学生或同行，在项目中共同成长。 Grow together as student, collaborator, or fellow practitioner.
