What is Yunisle?
中文 / Chinese first

寻屿 · Yunisle 是一个将发展心理学、自我决定理论与生活经验编织在一起的实践空间。 这里不是简单的“鸡汤”或“技巧收集”，而是一场缓慢而诚实的内在旅程： 看见自己如何被期待、如何习惯性地回应，以及如何一步步，长出属于自己的选择与声音。

English

Yunisle is a gentle space where developmental psychology, self-determination theory, and lived experience meet. It is not about quick fixes, but about an honest, step-by-step journey from being “the expected one” to becoming a self-awakened presence.
