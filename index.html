从“被期待的人”，走向“自我觉醒的存在”
From who you're expected to be to who you truly are

寻屿 Yunisle：
在云端，为自己找到一座岛 Yunisle:
A quiet isle in the clouds, for you

你是否也曾这样想过：
我不断满足他人的期待，却越来越听不见自己的声音。
努力工作、学习、照顾关系，却总在深夜怀疑——
“这真的是我想要的人生吗？”

Maybe this sounds familiar:
You keep meeting expectations, yet your own voice grows quieter.
You work, study, care for others – and still, late at night, you wonder:
“Is this the life I truly want?”

寻屿是一场共建的旅程。
我不是来“修好”你，而是和你一起：
从经验出发，看见自己的需要，理解那些纠缠的情绪，
一点点练习，用更诚实、更温柔的方式，与自己、与他人、与时间重新相遇。

Yunisle is a journey we co-create.
I'm not here to “fix” you. I'm here to walk with you:
starting from lived experience, sensing your needs, untangling your feelings, and practising, step by step, a gentler, more honest way of relating to yourself, others, and time.

一座介于云端与海面之间的心灵小岛。
这里尊重你的节奏，相信人的自我决定能力，
用心理学的精细、哲学的深度，陪你看见：
你一直都在，只是还没被温柔地叫到名字。

A small inner isle, somewhere between sea and clouds.
Here, your pace is respected, your capacity for self-determination is trusted.
With the precision of psychology and the depth of philosophy, we simply help you see:
you have been here all along – just not yet called by your own gentle name.

CO-CREATION · 共建 CO-CREATION · 共建

这不是“被治疗”，而是一场共建的练习 This is not “being treated”, but a practice we co-create

你带来真实，我带来框架 You bring your reality, I bring the framework
你带来故事、困惑、犹豫和渴望；我带来自我决定理论、发展心理学、来访者中心、人本与存在主义等理论的整合视角。 我们一起，把那些“说不清的感觉”放到可被看见的位置。
You bring your stories, doubts, hesitations, and longings. I bring an integrative lens: self-determination theory, developmental psychology, client-centred, humanistic and existential approaches. Together we place “those feelings you can’t quite name” where they can be seen.
从经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造 From experience → awareness → action → self-creation
不催促“立刻改变”，而是先允许你如实地体验；
在觉察中慢慢分辨什么是“我真正的需要”；
然后练习一点点可行的小行动，逐渐长出属于自己的生活方式。
We don’t rush into “instant change”. We begin by allowing your experience to be exactly as it is; then sharpen awareness of what you truly need, and practise small, realistic actions – until a life that feels like yours gradually takes shape.
邀请你，一起来共建寻屿 You’re invited to co-build Yunisle with me
无论你是来访者、研究者、教育工作者，还是正在寻找同行者的敏感灵魂， 都欢迎你加入这座“云端小岛”：提出问题、共创话题、发起小组、一起研究与实践。
Whether you’re a client, researcher, educator, or a sensitive soul seeking companions, you’re invited to this “isle in the clouds”: bring questions, co-create topics, launch groups, and weave research with lived life.
