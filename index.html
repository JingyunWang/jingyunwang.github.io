THE YUNISLE · INNER ISLES OF YUN

A quiet island in the clouds
for those who are tired of only being “who they should be”.

From the “expected self” to an awakening presence. Psychology as method, philosophy as bone, lived experience as sea. We walk a path from experience → awareness → action → self-creation.

Yunisle in one breath
  • A space to rebuild your relation with self, others, and time.
  • Where every step carries a story, echoing forward and shaping the soul of the journey.
  • Where research, counselling, and lived stories meet each other honestly.

Theory & Method

Psychology as method · Philosophy as spine · Inner isles as practice

Yunisle weaves together developmental psychology, self-determination theory, attachment and autonomy research, and a gentle existential lens. The work is not to “fix” people, but to help them listen, name, and meet their own lives with more honesty and freedom.

Guiding ideas

  • Self-Determination Theory: needs for autonomy, competence, and relatedness.
  • Experience → Awareness → Action → Self-creation as a living cycle.
  • From “the expected person” to “the one who can feel and choose.”

Lines I often return to

  • “When we are no longer able to change a situation, we are challenged to change ourselves.” — Viktor Frankl
  • “That which is damaged in relationship is healed in relationship.” — Relational traditions
  • “Autonomy is not isolation, but the courage to be a person among persons.” — Yunisle interpretation

Meet Yun

Researcher · Listener · Companion on the inner shore

I am Jingyun — a developmental psychologist with long-term experience in counselling, group work, and psycho-educational projects. I study autonomy, relationships, and intensive longitudinal data; I also sit with people in their grief, shame, and quiet hopes.

My work bridges research and lived experience. I care about how young people move from being “the child others expected” to becoming someone who can feel, choose, and create a life that makes sense to them.

Background

  • PhD in Developmental Psychology (Utrecht University).
  • Training in counselling and mental health education.
  • Over a decade of practice with individuals, groups, and workshops.

Activities so far

  • Workshops on autonomy, boundaries, and inner permission.
  • Reading groups on development, relationships, and self-compassion.
  • Research collaborations on peer victimization, rumination, and youth mental health.

Future directions

Yunisle will grow into a space for long-term inner work journeys, small circles of practice, and research-informed offerings that stay close to real life.

Topics & Projects

From the expected self to an awakening presence

理论与方法 Theory & Method

心理学为方法 · 哲学为骨架 · 生命经验为海

寻屿（Yunisle）结合发展心理学、自我决定理论、依恋与自主研究， 也吸收存在主义的视角。目标不是“修好”一个人，而是陪你更真实地 听见、命名、面对自己的生命——在自由与联结之间找到属于自己的位置。

核心线索

  • 自我决定理论：自主、胜任、联结三大心理需要。
  • 经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造 的循环练习。
  • 从“被期待的角色”走向“能感受、能选择的自己”。

我常常回望的一些话

  • “当我们无法改变处境时，我们被召唤去改变自己。”——弗兰克尔
  • “关系里受伤，在关系里疗愈。”——关系取向传统
  • “自主不是孤立，而是在众人之中仍能是一个完整的人。”——寻屿式理解

认识云 Meet Yun

研究者 · 倾听者 · 同行者

我是王静云，一名发展心理学研究者，也是一名长期实践心理咨询、 团体与心理教育工作的陪伴者。我研究自主性、人际关系与密集纵向数据， 也在一对一和小组中，陪人一起看见悲伤、羞耻、渴望和温柔。

话题与计划 Topics & Projects

从“被期待的人”到“自我觉醒的存在”

“寻屿”本身就是一场长程旅程： 帮助人从“别人期待的样子”，一步步靠近“能感受、能选择的自己”。 以心理学的方法、哲学的骨架，陪你在 经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造 的路径上， 修复、重建与自己、他人和时间的关系。

目前的主要话题

  • 如何与“期待”相处：家庭、文化与内在批评者。
  • 如何重新听见身体、情绪与直觉发出的微弱信号。
  • 如何设计一种既有自由感、又不失联结的生活方式。

可能的形式

  • 小型线上/线下共修小组与对话空间。
  • 关于自主性、发展与密集纵向数据的分享与讲座。
  • 结合写作、心理练习与温柔身体觉察的主题工作坊。
