寻屿 · Yunisle

云端与内在之岛 · 一起走向更自由、更真实的自己

在这里，“寻屿”不是一座外在的岛，而是每个人内心深处， 那片尚未被看见、却一直在呼唤的地方。

我是王婧芸（Jingyun），发展心理学研究者、心理实践者。 我相信：每一次停下脚步，每一次认真感受，都是在为自己缓缓建一座岛—— 可以暂时靠岸、可以重新呼吸、可以慢慢决定要如何继续生活。

也许，你正在经历这些：

  • 总在回应“别人期待的我”，却很难听见自己的声音；
  • 在人际、家庭或亲密关系中，常常感到冲突、愧疚或拉扯；
  • 理性上“都懂很多道理”，却依旧难以行动、难以真正松下来；
  • 知道自己需要休息和边界，但很难不自责。

寻屿 · Yunisle 想做的，是为你提供一个 可以诚实自处、可以练习选择、可以慢慢成为自己的空间。

这里以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架， 汇聚研究、书写、对话与实践，邀请你一起从 经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造， 重建与你自己、他人与时间的关系。

Yunisle — an inner isle of loving, becoming, and being whole.

Here, the “isle” is not a place on the map, but a quiet space within you — the part that has long been waiting to be seen, heard, and gently held.

I am Jingyun Wang, a developmental psychologist and practitioner. I believe that every pause, every honest feeling, is a small act of building your own island — a place to arrive, to breathe, and to choose how you wish to live.

Perhaps you know these moments:
moving through life by others’ expectations, feeling torn in relationships, understanding so much yet struggling to act or truly rest, wanting clearer boundaries without drowning in guilt.

Yunisle is an invitation to co-create an inner space where you can live more freely, feel more deeply, and meet who you truly are — through psychology as method and philosophy as backbone.

Together we walk a path from experience → awareness → action → self-creation, reshaping your relationship with self, others, and time.

云端之上，有一座只为你保留的岛。
你不必一下子抵达，只要每一次愿意靠近一点点。

Somewhere between cloud and sea, an island waits quietly within you. No rush to arrive — every step is already part of the journey.
