共建 · 不只是参与
Co-Creation · Beyond Participation

这里，不只是“你来听我说”

This Is Not “You Just Come and Listen”

寻屿是一场共建：你带着真实的生命经验，我带着心理学与哲学的框架， 我们一起去理解、命名、重写那些曾让你困住的故事。

Yunisle is co-created: you bring your lived experience, I bring psychological and philosophical frameworks, and together we make sense, give names, and re-write the stories that once held you back.

我们一起在做的三件事
Three Things We Do Together
  • 1 · 从经验出发，而不是从“应该”出发
    1 · Start from Experience, Not “Shoulds”

    愤怒、麻木、羡慕、嫉妒、失落……都是线索，不是错误。每一种感受，都指向你生命里的某个“被压抑的需要”。

    Anger, numbness, envy, jealousy, disappointment—none of these are mistakes. They are signals pointing toward unmet needs and forgotten parts of you.

  • 2 · 用理论搭骨架，而不是替你做决定
    2 · Use Theory as a Spine, Not a Cage

    以自我决定理论、发展心理学与存在主义哲学为骨架， 我们一起看见：你如何在关系与社会中，被塑造、也渴望重塑自己。

    With self-determination theory, developmental psychology, and philosophy as our backbone, we trace how you’ve been shaped by relationships and society—and how you long to reshape your own life.

  • 3 · 回到行动与创造，而不是停在“懂了”
    3 · Return to Action and Creation

    看懂只是开始。更重要的是：在现实限制之中，你仍然能迈出的那一小步。

    Insight is only the beginning. What matters is the next small, concrete step you can take—even within imperfect reality.
