Yunisle is an inner isle of loving, becoming, and being whole.
Here, every soul finds its own isle; every step carries a story that echoes forward and shapes who you are becoming.

Inner Isles · 心灵岛屿 Inner Isles · Heartland

你是否也曾这样问自己？

Have you ever asked yourself…

我在满足谁的期待？
如果不再只活在「应该」，我是谁？

Whose expectations am I carrying?
Who am I, if I am no longer living only in “shoulds”?

在关系里，总是照顾别人、压抑自己，却又害怕被看见真正的需要。
In relationships, you keep caring for others and silencing yourself, yet fear being truly seen in your needs.
在学业、工作与身份之间来回切换角色，却很少有机会问一句：
「那我真正想活成怎样的人？」
You switch roles between study, work, and family, rarely pausing to ask:
“Who is the person I truly wish to become?”
你渴望的是，不只是被修复，而是被理解、被陪伴，一起探索一条适合自己的路。
You long not only to be “fixed”, but to be understood, accompanied, and to co-create a path that is your own.

在这里，我们不是把你当成一个「问题」，而是当成一个正在书写中的故事。
我会用研究、实务与生命经验，和你一起，共同编织这座心灵岛屿。

Here, you are not a “problem” to be solved, but a story still being written.
With research, practice, and lived experience, we will co-weave this inner isle together.

也许，你正在经历——

Perhaps, this is where you are now—

· 总是活在他人的期待、标准和眼光里，久而久之，忘了自己想要什么。
· 理性上「都懂」，情绪却仍然起伏，被关系、家人、学业或工作牵动。
· 做了很多「自我提升」，却始终觉得行动和内心的期待对不上。

· You have been living in others’ expectations for so long that you can hardly remember what you truly want.
· You “understand everything” rationally, yet your emotions still surge with relationships, family, study, or work.
· You have tried many kinds of “self-improvement”, yet your actions and your inner longing never quite match.

这些，不是你「哪里不对」，而是你在很用力地，让自己在世界里好好生存下去。

None of this means something is “wrong” with you. It is evidence of how hard you have been trying to live in this world.

从「被期待的人」
到「自我觉醒的存在」

From “who I’m expected to be”
to “who I am becoming”

本项目是一场，从「别人眼中的我」，回到「我眼中的自己」的旅程。
我们以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架，把你的生命经验当成真正的教材： 从经验出发，看见感受与模式；在理解中生出领悟；再用温柔但稳固的行动，慢慢调整与自我、他人和时间的关系。

This project is a journey from “who I am in others’ eyes” back to “who I am to myself”.
Psychology is our method, philosophy our frame, and your lived experience is the real curriculum: we start from what you have gone through, make sense of your feelings and patterns, grow insight from understanding, and then translate it into gentle yet steady actions that change how you relate to yourself, others, and time.

你不需要再成为一个「更好的人」，而是一步步，成为更像自己的自己。

You are not here to become a “better person” in a generic sense, but to become more truly and fully yourself.

这不是一个「成品」空间

This is not a space for “finished products”

云之岛，更像一块正在生成中的岛屿。
我带来的，是研究与实践、工具与视角；你带来的，是真实的问题、生命故事与思考。

Yunisle is not a polished product, but an island in the making.
I bring research, practice, tools, and perspectives; you bring your questions, stories, and reflections.

我们一起共建这座岛：
让知识不只停留在纸上，而是落地到你的日常选择、关系互动与行动脚步里。

Together, we co-create this isle so that knowledge does not stay on the page, but sinks into your daily choices, relationships, and steps in life.

你可以在这里找到：

Here, you may find:

  • 以自我决定理论为核心的成长框架；
  • 从探索 → 领悟 → 行动 → 自我创造的具体路径；
  • 与前沿研究接轨、又贴近现实处境的对话与练习。
  • A growth framework grounded in Self-Determination Theory;
  • A concrete pathway from exploration to insight, action, and self-creation;
  • Conversations and practices connected to research yet deeply rooted in real life.
