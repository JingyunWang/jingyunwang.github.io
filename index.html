THEORY & PATHWAYS

Theory & Pathways from self-determination to self-creation

Yunisle is grounded in developmental psychology, self-determination theory, and a deep respect for lived experience. The work here is both gentle and rigorous.

Self-Determination Theory (SDT) Autonomy · Competence · Relatedness Experience → Insight → Action → Creation

“When we are no longer able to change a situation, we are challenged to change ourselves.” — Viktor Frankl

Core threads:

  • Self-Determination Theory: supporting autonomy, competence, and relatedness so that change grows from within, not from pressure.
  • Developmental lens: seeing you not as a fixed problem, but as a living process in motion.
  • Relational depth: healing happens in honest, attuned connection — with yourself and with others.

A simple pathway we keep returning to:

  • Experience · 经验： noticing what actually happens in your days.
  • Insight · 领悟： understanding the patterns, beliefs, and needs underneath.
  • Action · 行动： trying out new, aligned ways of responding.
  • Creation · 自我创造： gradually shaping a life that feels like it is truly yours.
理论与路径

理论与方法 · 从自我决定到自我创造 Theory & Pathways

寻屿的工作，以发展心理学、自我决定理论（Self-Determination Theory）， 以及对生命经验的尊重为基础——既温柔，也严谨。

自我决定理论（SDT） 自主 · 胜任 · 连接 经验 → 领悟 → 行动 → 自我创造

“当我们无法改变外在处境时，我们被邀请去改变自己。” —— 维克多·弗兰克尔

在这里，我们会做的事，大致包括：

  • 从「问题」到「发展中的人」： 不再把你看成需要被修理的对象，而是看见你正处在怎样的发展阶段与处境。
  • 支持你的自主性： 让改变的动力，尽可能来自你内在真正在乎的，而不是来自“应该”和“别人说”。
  • 回到关系里： 练习在关系中说真话、设界限，也练习允许自己被看见、被承接。

我们会一再回到的四个关键词：

  • 经验： 把你日常中“真的发生了什么”放在桌面上。
  • 意识 / 领悟： 看见背后的模式、信念、需求与情绪。
  • 行动： 尝试小而真实的新选择、新反应。
  • 自我创造： 一点点，把生活活成更像你自己的样子。
MEET YUN

Meet Yun researcher, listener, fellow human

I am Jingyun Wang — a developmental psychologist, researcher, and long-term psychological practitioner.

My work weaves together over a decade of counselling and group work, a PhD in developmental psychology, and an ongoing curiosity about how people grow in the space between autonomy and connection.

Areas I have walked in:

  • Adolescent and young adult autonomy in family, peer, and school contexts.
  • Intensive longitudinal designs and within-person dynamics.
  • Rumination, co-rumination, and youth mental health.
  • Workshops, reading groups, and group processes around shame, needs, and boundaries.

I do not promise quick fixes. I offer companionship, clarity, and a space where you do not have to pretend.

Yunisle is my way of bringing research, practice, and lived experience together — so that the distance between “knowing” and “living” can gently shrink.

TOPICS

Topics & Offerings from the expected self to the awakened self

Yunisle is not a fixed program, but a living set of conversations and spaces. Here are some of the themes I currently explore with people:

  • From “the expected one” to “the awakened self”: a journey to notice where you have been scripted, and where you long to rewrite.
  • Autonomy with roots: learning to choose without cutting yourself off from those you care about.
  • Inner isles & inner dialogues: listening to the parts of you that have been exiled, muted, or overworked.
  • Time, shame, and second chances: softening the inner critic and building a kinder future narrative.

Occasional groups, workshops, and reflective circles may be hosted. If you would like to be informed, you can write to me and share a few lines about what brings you here.

话题与活动

话题 · 活动 · 共建的空间 从「被期待的人」到「自我觉醒的存在」

寻屿不是一个固定的课程，而是一组不断生长的对话与场域。 以下是目前常出现的一些主题：

  • 从「被期待的人」到「在场的自己」： 看见那些被写进你生命剧本里的“必须”，以及你真正渴望的“可以”。
  • 带根的自主： 学习如何做选择，而不是“要么完全顺从、要么完全切断关系”。
  • 内在小岛与内在对话： 为那些被压抑、被过度用力、或长期被忽略的部分腾出空间。
  • 与时间和解： 面对羞耻、自责与“是不是已经太晚了”的焦虑， 练习为自己的人生写下不同的续篇。

不定期会有线上 / 线下小组、工作坊、阅读会等形式。 如果你愿意接收未来活动的信息，可以在「联系」中写信给我， 简单介绍你自己和此刻的处境。
