I’m Jingyun, a developmental psychologist and explorer of inner life. Here, I invite you to slow down, see yourself honestly, and walk alongside your own becoming.

也许，你正在经历
Perhaps you’re here because

总是在回应「别人的期待」，却越来越听不见自己的声音？

You keep meeting everyone else’s expectations,
and yet you can’t hear your own voice clearly?

  • 在家庭、伴侣或工作关系中，习惯照顾他人，而很少诚实对待自己的需要。
  • 理性上「都懂」，情绪却仍旧反复，卡在某些关系、选择或身份位置里。
  • 在学业、职业、迁移或人生重大转折点前，摇摆在「我要什么」与「我应该怎样」之间。
  • 做了很多努力和提升，却总感觉离真正的自己、想过的生活还有一段距离。
  • In family, partnership, or work, you’re used to caring for others, but rarely honest about your own needs.
  • You “understand everything” rationally, yet emotions keep looping around the same relationships and choices.
  • At turning points – study, career, migration, commitment – you swing between “What do I want?” and “What should I do?”
  • You’ve tried hard and grown a lot, but still feel a gap between your current life and the one that feels truly yours.

如果这些描述有一点触动你，那么欢迎你停留片刻。
寻屿不是要把你「修好」，而是陪你一起，看见你早已具备的力量。

If any of this feels familiar, you’re welcome to stay a little. Yunisle is not here to “fix” you, but to walk with you as you rediscover the strength already within you.

这不是一个「成品」空间
This is not a finished product

这里，更像一块正在生成中的岛屿。
我带来的，是研究与实践、工具与视角；你带来的，是真实问题、生命故事与思考。

我们一起共建这个岛，让知识不只停留在论文里，而是落地到你的日常选择、关系与行动里。

This place is more like an island in the making. I bring research, clinical and educational practice, and tools; you bring real questions, life stories, and reflections. Together we co-build this isle, so knowledge doesn’t stay in papers, but flows into your daily choices, relationships, and actions.
