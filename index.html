About

I am JY — a developmental psychologist shaped by many years of watching how people struggle, soften, heal, and rise again.

My work follows a simple, old truth: “When people feel seen, they change.”

I study autonomy, relationships, and the ways young people carry their stories inside their bodies and minds. My approach is scientific, but my heart stays with compassion, clarity, and the dignity of every person I meet.
