云端里的小岛 · 向内生长的旅程 A cloud island for inner growth

本项目是一场
从「被期待的人」到「自我觉醒的存在」的旅程。 This is a journey
from “the person others expect” to “the self that awakens from within”.

以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架，帮助你在 经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造 的路径中， 重新编织与自我、他人、命运的关系。 With psychology as method and philosophy as backbone, we walk a path of experience → awareness → action → self-creation, reweaving your relationship with self, others, and life.

寻屿 · YUNISLE —— 一个关于爱、成为与完整的内在之屿。 Yunisle — the inner island of loving, becoming, and being whole.

或许，你也正经历： Perhaps you are quietly carrying:

  • 在他人的期待、标准和评价中，慢慢忘记了「自己真正想要什么」。 Losing sight of what you truly want under expectations, standards, and evaluations.
  • 在关系、工作与角色之间来回切换，却很难感到踏实和被看见。 Moving between roles and relationships, yet rarely feeling grounded or seen.
  • 想要改变，却在犹豫、拖延、自我怀疑中打转，一再回到原点。 Wanting change, but circling in hesitation, procrastination, and self-doubt.

这不是一个「成品」空间 This is not a finished product

这里是一座正在生长中的心灵岛屿。
它由研究、故事、对话与实践组成，既有严谨的心理学方法，也有温柔的主观感受。

你不是来「被修好」，而是被邀请停一停，和自己一起，诚实地看见、命名并创造。 Yunisle is a living island, still growing.
It is made of research, stories, conversations, and lived practices— both rigorous psychological methods and tender inner experiences.

You are not here to be “fixed”. You are invited to pause, to see, to name, and to create—together with yourself.

  • 以自我决定理论为核心，搭建一套关怀内在动机的成长框架。 Rooted in Self-Determination Theory, we build a framework that cares for your inner motivations.
  • 从探索 → 领悟 → 行动，设计可实践、可停顿、可回望的微小路径。 From exploration → insight → action, we design gentle, practicable steps you can revisit.
  • 以研究者的严谨，与同行者的温度，在云端岛屿上和你并行。 With the rigor of a researcher and the warmth of a fellow traveler, we walk this cloud island with you.

为「共建」而生，而不是「被改造」 Built for co-creation, not correction

一起共建「寻屿 · Yunisle」 Co-create Yunisle together

如果你愿意，不只是「来听一场讲座」或「上一个课程」，而是愿意以研究者、实践者、 也以一个真实的人身份与我同行——
那么，你已经是这座小岛的一部分。 If you come not only as a “participant”, but also as a researcher, practitioner, and a real human being—then you are already part of this isle.

作为「共创者」而来 Come as a co-creator

参与研究项目、心理团体、共同写作与对话，贡献你的经验与观察， 让心理学走近真实的人生。 Join research projects, groups, and co-writing. Let your lived experience shape the way psychology meets real life.

作为「探索者」而来 Come as an explorer

带着你的困惑、好奇与尚未成形的问题，在这里慢慢整理， 找到属于自己的语言与方向。 Bring your questions, confusions, and unnamed feelings. Here, we take time to sort and give them language.

作为「见证者」而来 Come as a witness

见证自己的成长，也见证彼此的故事。让每一次相遇都成为 推动彼此前行的一束光。 Witness your own growth and each other’s stories. Each encounter becomes a small light moving us forward.
