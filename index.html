Home

Yunisle is a gently-lit space for those who are tired of being only “the expected one” – the good child, the reliable colleague, the strong friend – and are beginning to ask: “What would it mean to live as myself?”

This project is a journey from the “expected self” to an awakened, self-authored existence. Grounded in psychology and held by philosophy, it accompanies you from experience → awareness → action → self-creation, rebuilding your relationship with self, others, and time.

You are invited to bring your questions, weariness, hope, and unfinished stories. We walk slowly. We listen carefully. We let the tide of life become a place of learning, not just surviving.

Approach & Methodology

Yunisle rests on an integration of developmental psychology, self-determination theory, relational perspectives, and existential and phenomenological ways of seeing human experience.

Guiding questions

  • What does it mean to become more fully oneself, without abandoning connection?
  • How do autonomy, care, and belonging weave together in everyday life?
  • What stories are we living, often without knowing?

Theoretical roots

A few of the threads that hold this work:

  • Self-Determination Theory – we are nourished by autonomy, competence, and relatedness.
  • Attachment & relationships – we become ourselves in the presence of others.
  • Phenomenology – experience is not a mistake; it is data and poetry.
  • Existential & narrative perspectives – we are always already telling a story about who we are.

From exploration to action

The process can be held simply as:

Explore · 探索 Understand · 领悟 Act · 行动 Create · 自我创造

We begin with lived experience, stay long enough to understand, and then move – not towards perfection, but towards a life that feels more honest and alive.

Lines that stay close

“The curious paradox is that when I accept myself just as I am, then I can change.” – Carl Rogers

“Between stimulus and response there is a space. In that space is our power to choose our response. In our response lies our growth and our freedom.” – Viktor Frankl

首页

「寻屿 · Yunisle」是为那些活得很好、却渐渐感觉不到自己的人准备的— 那些一直扮演“懂事的孩子、可靠的同事、坚强的朋友”， 却开始隐隐发问：“如果不是别人期待的样子，我还可以是谁？”

这个项目是一场从“被期待的人”到“自我觉醒的存在”的旅程。 以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架， 陪你走过经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造的路径， 重新编织与自己、他人、时间的关系。

你可以带着困惑、疲惫、希望与未完成的故事来到这里。 我们不急着改变，而是学会看见：原来每一朵浪花，都算数。

方法与理念

在方法上，「寻屿」扎根于发展心理学、 自我决定理论、关系视角， 也受到存在主义与现象学的影响。

常常被问的几个问题

  • 怎样靠近自己的同时，不必切断与他人的联系？
  • 自主、亲密与归属，能否在日常生活里共存？
  • 我正在过着怎样的故事？这个故事是谁写的？

几个核心的支点

  • 自我决定理论：人需要自主感、胜任感与联结感，生命才会真正活起来。
  • 依恋与关系：我们总是在他人的回应中，一点点成为自己。
  • 现象学：经验不是错误，而是资料，也是诗。
  • 叙事与存在视角：我们总在讲述一个“我是怎样的人”的故事。

从经验，到行动

过程可以被放进一个简单的框架：

探索 领悟 行动 自我创造

不是为了成为“更完美”的人，而是更真实地在场， 更温柔地与自己站在一起。
