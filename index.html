从「被期待的人」到「自我觉醒的存在」 From what is expected of you, to who you are becoming

一个关于爱、成为与完整的 内在之屿 Yunisle, the inner island of loving, becoming, and being whole

我是 Jingyun，一名发展心理学研究者与同行者。
这里是一段向内生长的旅程——一座关于爱、存在与成为的岛屿，邀请你慢下来， 活得更自由，感受更深，在「被期待的人」与「真正的自己」之间，走完那段常被忽略的路。

I am Jingyun, a developmental psychologist and fellow traveller.
This is a journey of inward growth—an isle of love, being, and becoming. You are invited to slow down, live more freely, feel more deeply, and walk the often-forgotten path between “who you are expected to be” and who you truly are.

在寻屿 · Yunisle，我们以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架，
从经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造，温柔而清晰地重整你与自己、他人、时间与命运的关系。

At Yunisle, psychology is our method and philosophy our backbone.
We move from experience → awareness → action → self-creation, gently and clearly reweaving how you relate to yourself, others, time, and destiny.

云端里的小岛 · 心灵岛屿 An inner isle between cloud and sea

当你的内在小岛，缓缓浮上海面

When the inner isle slowly rises to the surface

你不必一次完成所有答案，只需要允许自己，每一次靠近一点点。

You don’t have to finish all the answers at once; you only need to allow yourself to come a little closer each time.

每个人心里都有一座小岛：那里藏着真实的渴望、未说出口的委屈，以及还没来得及照顾的自己。
Each of us carries an isle within us, where true longings, unspoken hurts, and the parts of us we have not yet tended quietly live.
在这里，研究、咨询与人生故事重新对话，让「你正在经历的生活」成为最重要的教材。
Here, research, therapeutic practice, and life stories meet again, turning the life you are already living into the central text.
你无需先变得更好，才值得被好好对待。此刻的你，就已经足以被温柔以待。
You do not need to become “better” first to deserve care. The you who is here now is already worthy of gentleness.

「每一步都带着故事的回响，塑造着旅程，也塑造着灵魂。」在寻屿，这些故事终于可以好好呼吸。

“Every step carries a story, echoing forward, shaping the soul of the journey.” At Yunisle, these stories finally learn how to breathe.

当你不再只是「被期待的人」，你还能成为谁？ If you were no longer only “who others expect”, who might you become?

你是否也曾这样问自己？

Have you ever asked yourself…

· 我的很多选择，是出于真心，还是在完成别人的剧本？
· 如果不再那么听话、那么努力，我还值得被爱吗？
· 总是为别人着想，那我自己，又在哪里？

· Are my choices truly mine, or am I mostly carrying out someone else’s script?
· If I stop being so compliant and hard-working, will I still be lovable?
· If I am always the one who understands and holds others, where am I in my own life?

有时，你或许也在心里轻轻地问过：
「如果不再只是别人期待的样子，我究竟，会是谁？」
这些问句本身，就是你开始靠近内在小岛的第一步。

At times you may have quietly wondered:
“If I am no longer only who others expect me to be, who could I become?”
These questions themselves are already your first steps toward the isle within.

一座为「正在转变中的人」预留的心灵小岛 An inner isle for those standing at the edge of change

关于「寻屿 · Yunisle」

About Yunisle

寻屿，是为那些长期活在「别人期待的样子」里，却开始渴望回到「真正的自己」的人而建的空间。
你也许正处在转型期——在国家、职业、身份、关系或人生阶段之间，既期待又不安。

Yunisle is a space for people who have spent years being “who they were expected to be” and now feel a quiet pull toward something truer. You may be in transition—between countries, careers, identities, relationships, or chapters of life.

在这里，心理学与哲学相遇，研究与真实生活相遇。
我们循着你的经验与故事，一起练习：重新聆听自己、照见模式、尝试新的选择，慢慢长出一种更自由、更诚实的活法。

Here, psychology meets philosophy, and research meets real life.
Following your own experiences and stories, we practice listening to yourself again, seeing your patterns, and experimenting with new choices—slowly growing a way of living that is freer and more honest.

这些不是「哪里不对」，而是你努力在世界生存的证据。 These are not flaws, but traces of how hard you have tried to live.

也许，你正在经历——

Perhaps, this is where you find yourself now—

· 总是活在他人的期待、标准和眼光里，久而久之，忘了自己真正想要什么；
· 在亲密关系、家庭、职场或跨文化生活中，一边讨好、一边想逃离；
· 理性上「都懂」，情绪却仍然起伏，被压力、焦虑或空心感牵着走；
· 做了很多「自我提升」，却始终觉得行动和内心的渴望对不上。

· Living for others’ expectations and standards for so long that you can barely remember what you truly want;
· In relationships, family, work, or cross-cultural life, you both please and want to run away;
· You “understand everything” in your head, yet your emotions still rise and fall with pressure, anxiety, or emptiness;
· You have tried many kinds of “self-improvement”, yet your actions and inner longing never quite match.

寻屿想做的，是陪你承认：你已经很努力了。然后，从这里出发，慢慢为自己长出新的可能。

Yunisle is here to help you first acknowledge how hard you have already tried—and then, starting from there, slowly grow new possibilities for yourself.

从「被定义的人」，走向「在场的自己」 From “defined by others” to “present as yourself”

从「被期待的人」到「自我觉醒的存在」

From “who you’re expected to be” to “a self that is awake”

这段旅程，不是把你变成一个「更完美的人」，而是一步步，让你可以更像自己。
你会学着看见：哪些是别人放在你身上的标签，哪些是你愿意为自己保留的方向， 并在一次次真实的选择里，练习把生命的主导权慢慢拿回手中。

This journey is not about turning you into a “more perfect person”, but about becoming, step by step, more like yourself.
You will learn to see which labels were placed on you and which directions you truly want to keep, practicing—through real, concrete choices—to reclaim authorship of your own life.

向内生长的旅程，爱、存在、成为之屿。 A journey inward—an isle of love, being, and becoming.

这不是一个「成品」空间

This is not a space for “finished products”

寻屿，更像一块正在生成中的岛屿。
我带来的是研究与实践、工具与视角；你带来的，是真实的问题、生命故事与细微的感受。 我们一起，让知识不只停留在纸上，而是落地在你每天的选择、关系与行动中。

Yunisle is an island in the making.
I bring research, practice, tools, and perspectives; you bring your questions, stories, and the subtle textures of your experience. Together, we make sure knowledge does not stay on the page, but finds its way into your everyday choices, relationships, and actions.

这里不是单向的「被辅导」，而是一个可以多重身份出现的共创空间：
· 你可以作为来访者，参与一对一对话、心理团体与长期旅程；
· 也可以作为合作者，一起设计项目、共写论文、开发实践与阅读计划；
· 你的每一次参与，都会反过来影响我如何设计未来的研究与课程。

This is not a place where you are only “worked on”, but a co-created space where you can appear in many roles:
· as a participant in one-to-one work, groups, or longer journeys;
· as a collaborator in designing projects, co-writing, or developing practices and reading paths;
· and your participation actively shapes how I design future research and offerings.

我想邀请你，不只是成为“更好的人”，而是成为「你自己」—— 在这里练习活得更自由、感受得更深，也更温柔地安放自己的内在之屿。

My invitation is not simply for you to become “better”, but to become more truly yourself—to live more freely, feel more deeply, and learn to gently anchor the isle within you.
