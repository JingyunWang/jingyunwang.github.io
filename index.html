从「被期待的人」到「自我觉醒的存在」 From what is expected of you, to who you are becoming

一个关于爱、成为与完整的 内在之屿 Yunisle, the inner island of loving, becoming, and being whole

我是 Jingyun，一名发展心理学研究者与同行者。
邀请你慢下来，活得更自由，感受更深，在「被期待的人」与「真正的自己」之间，走完那段常被忽略的路。

I am Jingyun, a developmental psychologist and fellow traveller.
I invite you to slow down, live more freely, feel more deeply, and walk the often-forgotten path between “who you are expected to be” and who you truly are.

在寻屿 · Yunisle，我们以心理学为方法，以哲学为骨架，
从经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造，重建你与自己、他人、时间与命运的关系。

At Yunisle, psychology is our method, philosophy our backbone.
We walk from experience → awareness → action → self-creation, rebuilding how you relate to yourself, others, time, and destiny.

云端里的小岛 · 心灵岛屿 An inner isle between cloud and sea

当你的内在小岛，缓缓浮上海面

When the inner isle slowly rises to the surface

这里不是要你立刻抵达，而是邀请你每一次只靠近一点点。

There is no rush to arrive. Every small step toward yourself is already part of the journey.

每个人心里都有一座小岛，那里藏着真实的渴望、未完成的故事和尚未被说出的问句。
Each of us carries an inner isle where true longing, unfinished stories, and unspoken questions quietly live.
在这里，研究、咨询与人生故事重新对话，让「你正在经历的生活」成为最重要的教材。
Here, research, conversations, and lived experience meet again, turning “the life you are living now” into the most important text.
你无需先变得更好，才值得被好好对待。此刻的你，就已经足够被温柔以待。
You do not have to become “better” first to deserve care. The you who is here now is already worthy of gentleness.

「每一步都带着故事的回响，塑造着旅程，也塑造着灵魂。」
在寻屿，这些故事终于可以好好呼吸。

“Every step carries a story, echoing forward, shaping the soul of the journey.”
Yunisle is where those stories learn to breathe.

一座关于爱、成为与完整的共建空间 A co-created isle of love, becoming, and wholeness

向内生长的旅程，
爱与存在的岛屿

A journey of inward growth,
an isle of love and being

「寻屿 · Yunisle」是一座安静的小岛，用来聆听、感受，并一点点改写我们正在活出的故事。
它属于那些在「别人眼中的样子」与「心里真实的声音」之间拉扯的人：厌倦只是扮演“被期待的人”， 却还在寻找「我究竟可以成为谁」的答案。

Yunisle is a quiet inner island where you listen, feel, and slowly rewrite the story you are living. It is for those who are tired of playing who they are “supposed” to be, yet are still searching for who they might truly become.

在这里，心理学是方法，哲学是骨架，真实经验是海洋。
我们从经验出发，走过 经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造 的路径， 让你的内在小岛，从零碎的片段，慢慢长成一座可以停靠、也可以再出发的完整之屿。

Here, psychology is our method, philosophy our backbone, and your lived experience the ocean we sail. We walk from experience to awareness, action, and self-creation, allowing your inner isle to grow from scattered fragments into a place where you can both rest and set sail again.

当你不再只是「被期待的人」，你还能成为谁？ If you were no longer only “who others expect”, who might you be?

你是否也曾这样问自己？

Have you ever asked yourself…

· 我现在的人生，是在回应谁的剧本？
· 如果不再只活在「应该」里，我还值得被爱吗？
· 总是为别人着想，那我自己，又在哪里？

· Whose script am I actually performing right now?
· If I stop living only by “shoulds”, will I still be lovable?
· If I am always the one who understands and holds others, where am I in my own life?

你或许曾在深夜问自己：
「如果不再只是别人期待的样子，我究竟，会是谁？」
这些问题不是你的缺陷，而是新的生活方式正在敲门的声音。

Perhaps you have quietly wondered at night:
“If I am no longer only who others expect me to be, who could I become?”
These questions are not flaws to erase; they are the sound of a different way of living knocking on your door.

这些不是失败，而是你努力与世界和解的证据 These are not failures, but traces of how hard you have tried

也许，你正在经历——

Perhaps, this is where you find yourself now—

从「被期待的人」到「自我觉醒的存在」

From “who you’re expected to be” to “who you are becoming”

· 长期活在“懂事、优秀、乖巧”的角色里，慢慢失去了与自己对话的能力；
· 在学业、职场、留学与跨文化生活中，把自己拆成许多版本，很难整合；
· 理性上懂了很多道理，却依旧在拖延、自责、完美主义与耗竭中打转；
· 在亲密关系与家庭里，一边照顾他人、一边想抽离，却很难安放自己的需要。

· Years of being “sensible, excellent, well-behaved” have slowly numbed your ability to truly talk with yourself;
· Across study, work, migration, and cross-cultural life, you feel split into many versions, unsure how to bring them together;
· You understand many things rationally, yet still circle around procrastination, self-blame, perfectionism, and exhaustion;
· In relationships and family, you care for everyone while quietly wanting to escape, never quite knowing where to put your own needs.

寻屿想陪你，看见这些都不是“你哪里不对”，而是一个人在努力与世界和解的全部证据。
我们会把它们写进故事，而不是只留在失眠的夜里。

Yunisle stands with you to see that none of this means “something is wrong with you”. They are proof of how hard you have tried to make peace with the world. Here, these experiences are written into your story, not left only to sleepless nights.

不只是成为“更好的人”，而是成为「你自己」 Not just “better”, but more truly yourself

这不是一个「成品」空间

This is not a space for “finished products”

邀你一起共建

You are invited to co-create

寻屿更像一块正在生成中的岛屿。
我带来的是研究与实践、工具与视角；你带来的，是疑惑、故事与生命经验。 我们一起，让知识不只停留在论文和概念里，而是落地到你的日常选择、关系互动与行动脚步上。

Yunisle is an island in the making. I bring research, practice, tools, and perspectives; you bring your questions, stories, and lived experience. Together, we make sure knowledge does not stay in papers and concepts, but sinks into everyday choices, relationships, and the way you move through the world.

这里不是单向的「被辅导」，而是一个可以多重身份出现的场域：
· 你可以是来访者、写作者、研究参与者、合作者；
· 我们可以一起设计工作坊、心理团体、研究项目与阅读练习；
· 让每一次对话，既回应当下的困惑，也悄悄塑造更长远的生命路径。

This is not a place where you are merely “worked on”, but a field where you can show up in many roles:
· as a participant in conversations or groups,
· as a co-writer or research collaborator,
· as someone experimenting with new ways of living.
Every encounter responds to what you are facing now, while quietly shaping the path ahead.

我想邀请你，不只是成为“更好的人”，而是一步步，成为「你自己」。

My invitation is not for you to become a vaguely “better” person, but to become, step by step, more deeply yourself.
