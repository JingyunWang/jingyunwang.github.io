Yunisle · The inner isles of loving, becoming, and being whole.

寻屿 · 在云与海之间，找回那个正在成为的自己。

I am Jingyun, a developmental psychologist and companion on inner journeys. Yunisle is a quiet space where we listen, feel, and slowly re-shape the stories we live in.

我是 王静云，发展心理学研究者，也是在场的同行者。 「寻屿 Yunisle」是一座安静的小岛，用来聆听、感受，并一点点改写我们正在活出的故事。

Have you ever felt like you were living as “the expected one” instead of the person you quietly long to be?

你是否也曾觉得自己活成了「别人期待的样子」， 却离那个真正渴望成为的自己，越来越远？

Yunisle invites you to move from expectation to awakening — from merely coping, to creating a life that feels like yours.

「寻屿」邀请你从「被期待的人」走向「自我觉醒的存在」， 从只是撑着活下去，到 真正活成属于自己的样子。

Every step carries a story, echoing forward, shaping the soul of the journey.

每一步都带着故事的余音， 在时间里回响，慢慢塑造旅程的灵魂。

Yunisle weaves psychology, philosophy, and lived experience into a gentle framework: from experience → awareness → action → self-creation.

在这里，心理学、哲学与亲身经验被织进一套温柔而清晰的路径： 从经验 → 觉察 → 行动 → 自我创造。

This is an inner island where you can live more freely, feel more deeply, and come closer to who you truly are.

这是一座可以暂时靠岸的小岛， 让你活得更自由，感受得更深，并靠近那个更真实的自己。

Theory & Pathways

理论与路径 · Theory & Pathways

Yunisle rests on a few steady anchors: Self-Determination Theory, humanistic and existential psychology, attachment and relational work, and the slow philosophy of becoming.

「寻屿」扎根于几块稳固的基石： 自我决定理论，人本与存在主义心理学，依恋与关系视角， 以及关于「人成其为人」的哲学思考。

Self-Determination Theory · SDT

自我决定理论 · SDT

We all long to feel autonomous, competent, and connected. When these needs are supported, life feels more alive and meaningful.

人都渴望三件事：自主、胜任、联结。 当这些基本需要被支持，我们的生命才真正有「活着」的感觉。

Exploration · Insight · Action

探索 · 领悟 · 行动

The Yunisle path invites you to move from raw experience, to gentle awareness, to experiments in action, and finally to self-authored ways of being.

「寻屿」的路径，就是从经验出发， 走向温柔而清晰的觉察，再到小小的行动实验， 最终慢慢活出自己写下的生命方式。

Voices that guide this isle

这座小岛背后的声音

  • Humanistic psychology — every person carries an inner tendency toward growth when met with empathy and honesty.
  • 人本主义心理学 —— 当一个人被真诚与共情地看见，内在的成长力量就会慢慢苏醒。
  • Existential thought — we cannot avoid freedom, responsibility, and meaning; we can only learn to carry them more wisely.
  • 存在主义 —— 我们无法逃离自由、责任与意义，只能学会更有智慧地背负它们。
  • Relational & attachment work — we become ourselves in relationship; healing also happens in safe, honest contact.
  • 依恋与关系视角 —— 我们在关系中被塑造，也会在被安全、诚实地相遇中慢慢复原。

Meet Yun · About Me

遇见云 · 关于我

I am Jingyun Wang — a developmental psychologist, counsellor, and long-time companion of inner work. My research centers on autonomy, relationships, youth development, and intensive longitudinal designs.

我是 王静云，发展心理学博士，长期从事心理咨询、团体与心理教育工作。 我的研究聚焦于：自主性发展、亲密与同伴关系、青少年心理健康，以及密集纵向研究设计。

For over a decade, I have moved between research rooms, counselling rooms, and group circles — witnessing how people struggle, soften, and grow.

十多年里，我在研究室、咨询室和团体空间之间穿梭， 见证人们在挣扎、松动与成长中的细微变化。

Yunisle is my way of weaving these worlds together: science with tenderness, structure with soul.

「寻屿 Yunisle」是我把这些世界织在一起的方式： 用科学守护温柔，用结构承载灵魂。

Topics & Journeys

话题与旅程

Yunisle is a long-term project: a journey from being “the expected one” to becoming an awakened, self-creating presence.

「寻屿」是一个长期计划： 从「被期待的人」，走向「自我觉醒、能为自己创造人生的存在」。

Through workshops, writing, and groups, we rebuild our relationship with: self, others, and time.

通过工作坊、写作和小组，我们一起重新编织 与自我、他人与时间的关系。

From expectation to awakening 从被期待到自我觉醒

Naming the roles you have carried, and meeting the person beneath them.

看见那些一直扮演的角色，也看见角色之下那个真实的人。

Inner isles & relationships 内在小岛与关系

How we protect, distance, or open our inner islands in close relationships.

在亲密关系中，我们如何守护、退缩或打开自己的「小岛」。

Time, shame, and second chances 时间、羞耻与重来一次

Working with regret and shame while still moving forward with gentleness.

带着羞耻与遗憾，仍能温柔地继续向前。

Making room for your own life 为自己的人生留出空间

Small, concrete steps for living more freely and more truly as yourself.

通过细小而具体的行动，一点点活得更自由、更像自己。
