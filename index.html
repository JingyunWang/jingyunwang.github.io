为“转型期”的你，搭一条回到自己的路 Jingyun Wang｜A clear path back to yourself—especially in transitions

我做三件事：用心理学研究的证据，用咨询与团体的方法， 陪你在过渡期/转型期把混乱理清——从“被推着走”，走向更有主体性的选择。

I do three things: bring evidence from psychology research, use methods from counselling & groups, and support you through life transitions—from drifting to choosing with agency.

这不是“变得完美”的训练营。我们给自己喘息的时间，把根扎稳—— 情绪与需求、关系与边界、自主性与行动。然后你自然会走得更远。

This is not a bootcamp for becoming “more impressive”. It’s the classical work: emotions & needs, boundaries & relationships, autonomy & action.

适合谁｜你可能正在经历 For whom｜You may be here because
  • 身份在变：毕业/换城市/跨文化/换行业，心里却更不确定。
  • 关系在卡：总是体谅别人，自己的需要却没地方站。
  • 做选择很难：明明“都懂”，却行动不了，像被压力牵着走。
  • 想要更真实：不想再只当“被期待的人”。
  • Your identity is shifting: moves, cultures, careers—certainty fades.
  • Relationships feel stuck: you care for others, your needs disappear.
  • Choices feel heavy: insight exists, action doesn’t follow.
  • You want to live more truthfully, not just by expectations.
你会在这里得到什么 What you’ll get here

少一点空话，多一点可走的路：把“自我探索”落到具体选择与可持续的关系里。

Less slogans, more steps: self-exploration that lands in choices and relationships you can sustain.

PATH 01PATH 01
把转型期“理清”Clarify transitions

识别你被困住的循环：讨好/拖延/自责/空心感，把问题从“我不行”改写成“我需要什么”。

Name the loop (people-pleasing, guilt, numbness) and shift from “I’m broken” to “what do I need?”.

PATH 02PATH 02
练习主体性Practice agency

用“自主性/边界/承诺”的语言，做出更像自己的选择——不是硬撑，是更清醒。

Choose with autonomy, boundaries, commitment—less force, more clarity.

PATH 03PATH 03
让关系能呼吸Let relationships breathe

在人际关系里既能靠近也能说“不”：把共情沟通变成可用的句子与练习。

Stay close without losing yourself: empathy + concrete phrases you can actually use.
