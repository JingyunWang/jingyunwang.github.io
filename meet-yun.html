Hi, I’m Yun.
I grew up in China and completed my PhD in developmental psychology in Europe.
Over the past decade, I have walked alongside people in therapy rooms, groups, university classrooms and research projects — from adolescents to early-career researchers, teachers and parents.

熟悉与擅长的话题

Topics I work with most

  • 如何从「听话好学生」过渡到「有边界的成年人」
  • 与父母、伴侣、导师建立更清晰但不冷漠的关系
  • 拖延、自责、内耗背后的需要与动力
  • 跨文化学习、移居与身份认同的摇摆
  • Moving from “good, obedient student” to a bounded, grounded adult
  • Building clearer yet warm relationships with parents, partners and mentors
  • Needs and motivations behind procrastination, self-blame, and burnout
  • Cross-cultural study, migration, and the wavering of identity

未来愿景：让研究长出日常的形状

Vision: letting research grow into everyday forms

我希望，未来几年能和更多人一起，共建这样一种可能：
· 心理学不只是危机时才会用到的“救火工具”，而是日常生活的思考方式；
· 研究者不只写论文，也走进真实情境，与个体和系统对话；
· 你不只是接受服务的“来访者”，也是一起构思、一起验证新路径的合作者。

In the coming years, I hope to co-create with more people a possibility where:
· Psychology is not only for emergencies, but a way of thinking in daily life;
· Researchers not only publish papers, but also enter real contexts and talk with individuals and systems;
· You are not only a “client”, but a co-thinker and co-creator of new pathways.
