遇见云 · About Yun

一个在云端小岛上慢慢行走的人
中文

我是王静云（Yun），发展心理学博士，长期在青少年发展、家庭与同伴关系、 自主性和心理健康的交界处工作，也在咨询室、课堂和各类工作坊中，聆听人与自己和世界和解的故事。

我相信，每个人都有一座属于自己的小岛：那里不完美，却真实；不喧闹，却有生命的流动。 寻屿 · Yunisle 是我邀请你一起登陆这座小岛的方式。

English

I am Jingyun Wang (Yun), a developmental psychologist working at the intersections of youth development, family and peer relationships, autonomy, and mental health. My work lives in research, teaching, counselling, and group spaces.

I believe each person carries an inner isle — imperfect yet real, quiet yet alive. Yunisle is my invitation for you to land there, gently, and see what might unfold.
