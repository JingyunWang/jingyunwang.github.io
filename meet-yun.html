走近云 · 谁在这座岛屿上点灯 Meet Yun · The one tending the island light

我是 Jingyun —— 心理学研究者、咨询实务者，也是一名长期的自我探索者。 寻屿，是我在研究与人生的交界处，为「被期待的人」预留的一座内在小岛： 你可以暂时放下「要表现好」，重新练习，如何以更自由、更诚实、更温柔的方式活下去。

I’m Jingyun—a psychologist, practitioner, and long-term explorer of my own inner world. Yunisle is an inner island I’ve set aside at the intersection of research and life, for those who have spent years being “who they were expected to be”: a place to put down performance for a while, and practice living with more freedom, honesty, and gentleness.

这里的每一次对话、每一个项目，既扎根于严谨的科研，也尊重你作为一个 真实的人，而不是某个标签或诊断。

Every conversation and project here is grounded in rigorous research, while treating you as a whole person, not a label or diagnosis.

出生于新疆石河子，辗转中国与欧洲，在不同文化与教育体系中生活、学习与工作。 这些迁徙，让我比起「完美答案」，更相信 人与自己和解、与世界重新对话的过程。

I was born in Xinjiang, China, and have lived, studied, and worked across China and Europe. These crossings taught me to trust not in perfect answers, but in the process of making peace with oneself and re-negotiating with the world.

我是谁 · 几个简单的标签 Who I am · A few simple labels

学历上，我是发展心理学博士， 研究青少年在家庭、同伴与学校情境中的 自主性、关系与心理健康；
工作上，我是心理实践者、团体促动者与成长同行人， 带领过多场心理团体、读书会与工作坊； 人生上，我则是一名在不同城市与身份之间迁徙的人， 一直在练习如何在多重期待中，活得更像自己。

In academia, I’m a developmental psychologist, studying adolescents’ autonomy, relationships, and mental health in families, peer groups, and schools.
In practice, I’m a practitioner, group facilitator, and companion in growth, having led groups, workshops, and reading circles. In life, I’m someone who has moved across cities and roles, continuously practicing how to live more like myself amid many expectations.

如果要用一句话概括我在做的事：
用心理学与哲学，把「我是谁」「我要怎样活」这两个问题，变成一条可以一起走的路。

If I had to summarise my work in one line:
I use psychology and philosophy to turn “Who am I?” and “How do I want to live?” into a path we can walk together.

正式训练与资质，给了我一套严谨的「看世界的镜头」。
Formal training and qualifications give me careful lenses through which to see your world.

  • 中国心理学与心理健康相关学位背景，熟悉中西教育体系与文化语境。

这些经历，并不是为了证明我「足够好」，而是为了说明： 我习惯在复杂、真实的情境中工作。
These experiences are not to prove I am “good enough”, but to show that I am used to working in complex, real-life contexts.

在研究里，我学习如何用数据与理论，诚实地面对人类发展的曲折与不完美； 在实践与教学里，我学习如何和真实的学生、来访者、同事一起， 把「科学」落地到一个个具体的决定与转折点上。

In research, I learn to face the twists and imperfections of human development with data and theory. In practice and teaching, I learn to translate “science” into concrete choices and turning points with students, clients, and colleagues.

这些土壤，让我在陪你看自己时，既不过度乐观，也不轻易绝望—— 而是陪你一起，把复杂活成可以拿在手里的下一步。

This soil helps me stay neither naively optimistic nor easily hopeless when we look at your life—rather, to help you turn complexity into a tangible next step.

我关注的议题 · 如果你在这些问题里看见自己 What I care about · If you see yourself in these questions

很多来到寻屿的人，都在身份、关系与方向之间摇摆： 一边努力扮演「合格的大人/学生/子女/伴侣」，一边又隐隐觉得， 有一个更真实的自己还没被安放好。

Many people who come to Yunisle are standing between identities, relationships, and directions: trying to be a “good adult / student / child / partner”, while sensing that a more authentic self has not yet found a home.

在真实生活里练习 · 团体与空间 Practicing in real life · Groups & spaces

在过去的几年里，我带领或共同发起过不同形式的空间：

Over the years, I’ve led or co-created different kinds of spaces:

  • 以自我探索与人际关系为主题的心理团体与长期小组；
  • 围绕动机、自主性与成长主题的工作坊、读书会与对谈；
  • 在大学环境中，为学生与青年提供过渡期的陪伴与支持。
  • Psychological groups and long-term circles on self-exploration and relationships;
  • Workshops, reading circles, and conversations on motivation, autonomy, and growth;
  • Support for students and young adults navigating transitions in university settings.

对我来说，「安全的空间」不是一个口号，而是一种细致的练习： 在其中，我们学着坦诚而不过度裸露，学着照顾自己，也照顾彼此。

For me, a “safe space” is not a slogan but a careful practice: a place where we learn to be honest without feeling over-exposed, to care for ourselves while caring for one another.

正在长成的愿景 · 这座岛，还会去向哪里 Growing visions · Where this isle is heading

寻屿不是一个「已经完工」的品牌，而是一块还在长出的岛屿。 未来，我希望和你一起，把它慢慢长成这样：

Yunisle is not a finished brand, but an island still growing. In the future, I hope to grow it together with you into something like this:

  • 一个可以用中英文对话的空间，让不同文化中的经验，都能被看见与对话；
  • 兼具研究深度与人味的项目：让论文与真实生活互相滋养，而不是彼此脱节；
  • 长期的内在旅程与共创工作坊，陪你在关键转折期，做出更像你自己的选择；
  • 一个可以让「被期待的人」慢慢卸下铠甲，练习成为「在场的自己」的心灵岛屿。
  • A bilingual space where experiences from different cultures can meet and be heard;
  • Projects that hold both research depth and human warmth, so papers and real life nourish each other;
  • Longer inner journeys and co-created workshops to accompany you through key transitions;
  • A harbour where those “living by expectations” can slowly lay down their armour and practice being fully present as themselves.

如果你愿意，你不只是「来访者」，也可以是这座岛的 共建者、见证者与讲述者。

If you’d like, you’re not only welcome as a “client”, but also as a co-creator, witness, and storyteller of this isle.
