Inner Compass

How we navigate the inner isles

Yunisle is grounded in Self-Determination Theory, developmental psychology, and a pluralistic view of psychotherapy. We care about autonomy, connection, and competence — not as buzzwords, but as lived experiences.

Self-Determination Theory

Autonomy, relatedness, competence

In simple words: people grow best when they can choose with integrity, belong without losing themselves, and feel able to meet what life asks of them.

“We are not only the product of our past, but also the author of our meanings.”

Process

Explore · Make sense · Act · Create

In Yunisle, we often move in cycles:

  • Explore — noticing experience with curiosity, not judgment.
  • Make sense — connecting dots, finding language, naming patterns.
  • Act — gentle experiments in boundaries, choices, and relationships.
  • Create — slowly shaping a life that feels more like your own.
