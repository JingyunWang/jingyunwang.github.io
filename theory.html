自我决定理论 · Self-Determination Theory Self-Determination Theory

我长期在发展心理学与动机研究中工作，自我决定理论（SDT）是寻屿的重要底层支撑。

· 自主性：不是“我想干嘛就干嘛”，而是可以诚实地对自己说——这真的是我认同的选择。
· 胜任感：不仅是“我做得好不好”，更是“我有没有机会在重要的领域里，一点点变好”。
· 联结感：在关系中不必隐藏自己，也不需要讨好，仍然可以被看见、被尊重。 My academic work lives in developmental psychology and motivation research, and Self-Determination Theory (SDT) is one of the core pillars of Yunisle.

· Autonomy: not “I do whatever I want”, but being able to say, “I can stand behind this choice.”
· Competence: not just “Am I good enough?”, but “Do I have room to grow in what matters to me?”
· Relatedness: being able to stay in relationship without hiding or pleasing away your self.

探索 · 领悟 · 行动 Exploration · Insight · Action
一 · 探索 1 · Exploration

从故事出发，不急着给答案。我们先允许混乱存在，慢慢看见：这些年，我是如何一路走到这里的？ We start from your stories, not from answers. We allow the mess to be there, and carefully see: how did I walk myself here?

二 · 领悟 2 · Insight

借助心理学理论、发展视角与哲学思考，把“情绪风暴”翻译成可以被理解的结构：模式、动力、需求、边界。 Using psychological theories, developmental lenses and philosophical thinking, we translate emotional storms into patterns, dynamics, needs, and boundaries.

三 · 行动 3 · Action

不追求“彻底改变”，而是找一两步可行的微行动：对话方式的一点点调整，对自己说话语气的一点点变化。 We don’t chase “complete change”, but look for one or two realistic micro-actions: slight shifts in how you speak to others, and to yourself.
