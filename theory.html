自我决定理论 · Self-Determination Theory

Self-Determination Theory

自我决定理论（Deci & Ryan）指出，人之所以能持续成长，不是因为被逼得足够狠， 而是因为三种基本心理需求被真实地满足： 自主（Autonomy）、胜任（Competence）、联结（Relatedness）。

Self-Determination Theory (Deci & Ryan) suggests that growth does not happen because we are pressured hard enough, but because three basic psychological needs are genuinely met: autonomy, competence, and relatedness.

“人类天生倾向于成长，但这种倾向只有在支持性的环境中才会展开。” —— Edward Deci & Richard Ryan
“Humans have an inherent tendency toward growth, but it flourishes only in supportive conditions.” — Edward Deci & Richard Ryan

生态系统理论 · 你不是独自形成的

Ecological Systems Theory · You Are Not Formed Alone

布朗芬布伦纳提醒我们：一个人的困惑、挣扎或停滞， 往往不是“个人不够努力”，而是长期身处在 不支持真实自我的关系与结构中。

Bronfenbrenner reminds us that many struggles are not due to personal weakness, but to prolonged exposure to systems and relationships that do not support the expression of one’s real self.

“为了理解一个人，你必须理解他所处的整个生态环境。” —— Urie Bronfenbrenner
“To understand a person, one must understand the entire ecological system in which they live.” — Urie Bronfenbrenner
