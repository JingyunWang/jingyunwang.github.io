ROOTS & PATHWAYS · 理论与方法 ROOTS & PATHWAYS · Theory & Method

让理论为生命服务，而不是让生命去迎合理论 Theory in service of life, not life squeezed into theory

在寻屿，理论不是冷冰冰的名词，而是帮助我们理解自己的一束束光。
我主要整合以下视角，与来访者共同走过“探索 — 领悟 — 行动”的路径。

At Yunisle, theory is not a cold list of terms. It is light – helping us see ourselves more clearly.
I mainly integrate the following lenses, to support a shared process of “exploration – insight – action”.

SELF-DETERMINATION THEORY
自我决定理论：关系、安全感与选择的自由 Self-Determination Theory: Relatedness, safety, and choice

自我决定理论认为，人天生有三大基本心理需要：自主、胜任、联结。
我们会一起看：你的生活中，这三种需要怎样被满足，也怎样被忽视？
当你重新拥有“可以选择”的感觉时，很多卡住的地方，会自然松动。

Self-Determination Theory suggests humans have three basic psychological needs: autonomy, competence, and relatedness.
We will look together: how are these needs met – and unmet – in your life? When the sense of “I can choose” returns, many stuck places begin to soften on their own.

HUMANISTIC
人本主义：你已经拥有成长的种子 Humanistic: You already carry the seed of growth

Rogers 曾说：“当我接纳自己如其所是，我就开始改变。”
在安全、尊重和真实的关系里，我们不急着修正你，而是允许你先“做自己”—— 在这个基础上，改变才有可能是出于内在，而不是单纯地讨好世界。

Rogers wrote: “The curious paradox is that when I accept myself just as I am, then I can change.”
Within a safe, respectful, and genuine relationship, we don’t rush to correct you. We first allow you to be. From there, change can grow from within, rather than from pleasing the world.

EXISTENTIAL
存在主义：在有限中，活出自己的回应 Existential: Your response within the finite

存在主义提醒我们：孤独、自由、死亡、意义的缺席，这些不是“病”，而是人的共同命题。
我们会一起思考：在无法回避的不确定中，你想如何回应？你的价值观，如何真实地落在生活细节里？

Existential approaches remind us: loneliness, freedom, death, and the absence of ready-made meaning are not “illnesses”, but shared human conditions.
We will ask: given this unavoidably uncertain world, how do you wish to respond? How might your values become embodied in the details of daily life?

DEVELOPMENTAL & SYSTEMIC
发展与家庭系统：理解你是如何走到今天 Developmental & systemic: How you came to be here

很多“现在的自己”，其实是过去为了生存和被爱而练习出的模式。
发展心理学与家庭系统视角，帮助我们尊重这些模式曾经的保护作用，同时温柔地问： “在今天，它们是否依然适合你？”

Many patterns of “who I am now” were once creative strategies for survival and love.
Developmental and family-systems lenses help us honour how these patterns once protected you, while gently asking: “Are they still serving you today?”

EXPLORATION · INSIGHT · ACTION EXPLORATION · INSIGHT · ACTION

从设问，到看见，再到行动的小步练习 From questions to seeing, to small, grounded actions

阶段一 · 探索 Phase 1 · Exploration
  • 把“说不清”的体验，用安全的语言慢慢讲出来。
  • 学会区分：哪些是他人的期待，哪些是自己的声音。
  • Put vague, tangled experiences into words in a safe space.
  • Learn to separate external expectations from your own voice.
阶段二 · 领悟 Phase 2 · Insight
  • 从理论出发，看清“原来我是这样应对孤独 / 冲突 / 不确定”。
  • 理解：这些模式曾经如何保护我，也在现在如何限制我。
  • See, through theory, how you’ve been responding to loneliness, conflict, and uncertainty.
  • Understand how these patterns once protected you – and how they now limit your choices.
阶段三 · 行动与自我创造 Phase 3 · Action & self-creation
  • 不追求“完美改变”，只练习细小却真实的行为调整。
  • 在一次次试探中，慢慢长出“属于我的生活节奏与选择”。
  • No perfect makeovers – only small, honest behavioural experiments.
  • Through repeated tries, a way of living that feels like your own quietly emerges.
