理论与路径 · 这座岛屿的骨架 Theory & Methods · The bones of this isle

在寻屿，我们不会用艰涩的专业词来「讲理论」，而是把理论当作 照亮生活的灯： 让你知道，自己现在的样子，并不是「哪儿不对」，而是有心理学、发展与环境脉络可循。

At Yunisle, theory is not a wall of jargon but a lamp over real life: a way to see that how you are now is not “wrong”, but understandable within psychological, developmental, and ecological contexts.

这里的工作，大致站在三个支点上： 自我决定理论（你如何被支持、被限制）、 生态系统视角（你身处怎样的世界）、以及 多流派对话（不同心理学家的智慧，在你身上相遇）。

Our work rests on three main pillars: Self-Determination Theory (how you are supported or constrained), an ecological lens (the worlds you move through), and dialogue across traditions (letting different thinkers speak to your life).

这不是抽象理论，而是你生活的地图 Not abstract theory, but a map of your life
当你看懂「发生了什么」，改变就不再只靠意志力。
When you understand what’s happening, change no longer rests only on willpower.

我们会一起，把你正在经历的困惑、关系与抉择，放回到发展阶段、关系经验与社会语境里。 理论负责提供结构，而你负责提供真实的生活。

Together, we place your confusion, relationships, and decisions back into developmental stages, relational histories, and social context. Theory offers structure; you offer real life.

自我决定理论：三大心理需要，不只是「努力不够」 Self-Determination Theory: Three needs beyond “trying harder”

自我决定理论（Self-Determination Theory, SDT）提醒我们：一个人是否有动力、是否能向内生长， 不只是「够不够自律」，而是看他的核心心理需要是否被看见与支持。

Self-Determination Theory (SDT) shows that our motivation and growth are not just about “discipline”, but about whether our basic psychological needs are seen and supported.

自主 Autonomy
「我是否可以，活成比较像自己的样子？」
“Do I have room to live in a way that feels like me?”
  • 可以表达不同意见，而不总是担心「不乖」「不懂事」。
  • 重要决定不只是被安排，而是可以参与、可以选择。
  • 知道自己正在做的事，对自己的人生有意义。
  • Being able to disagree without always fearing you’re “too much” or “not good enough”.
  • Having a say in important decisions, not only being assigned a role.
  • Feeling that what you’re doing matters to your own life, not only to others’ expectations.
胜任 Competence
「我是否有机会，一点点变得更有把握？」
“Do I have chances to grow my sense of capability?”
  • 有可以循序渐进练习的空间，而不是只被要求「一次做到完美」。
  • 有人看见你的努力过程，而不只是结果和分数。
  • 可以尝试、可以犯错，错误不会直接等于「我不行」。
  • Having space to practice gradually, not just pressure to get it “perfect” at once.
  • Having your effort noticed, not only your outcomes and scores.
  • Being allowed to try and fail without every mistake meaning “I’m a failure”.
连接 Relatedness
「在关系里，我是否可以既被爱，也保留自己？」
“Can I feel loved and still be myself in relationships?”
  • 有可以诚实说出脆弱与需要的关系，而不总是担心被嫌弃。
  • 在家人、伴侣、同事、同学面前，可以逐渐做回真实的自己。
  • 不再只用讨好、隐忍、照顾别人，来换取被留在关系里的资格。
  • Having relationships where you can show vulnerability and needs without constant fear of rejection.
  • Gradually being able to show your real self with family, partners, colleagues, and friends.
  • Not relying solely on pleasing, over-caring, or self-erasure to stay in connection.

在寻屿，我们不会只看「你做不到什么」，而是一起梳理： 这些心理需要，在你的成长里是如何被支持、被压抑、又如何慢慢被你捡回来的。

At Yunisle, we don’t only ask “What can’t you do?” but trace how these needs have been supported, suppressed, and reclaimed across your life.

不只是「我有问题」：生态系统视角 Not just “my problem”: an ecological lens

发展心理学家 Bronfenbrenner 提醒我们： 一个人从来不是「孤立存在」，而是活在多层系统的交织里——家庭、学校、组织、文化与时代。 很多你以为是「我的性格问题」，其实与整个生态系统的支持与限制有关。

Developmental psychologist Bronfenbrenner reminds us: no one exists in isolation. We live within multiple, layered systems—family, school, organisations, culture, and history. Much of what feels like a “personal defect” is deeply tied to how these systems support or constrain us.

把视角从「我哪里不行」移向「我身处怎样的环境」 Shifting from “What’s wrong with me?” to “What context am I in?”

在寻屿，我们会一边看你的内在感受，一边看你所处的外在环境： 能否支持你的自主？是否认可你的价值？是否给过你试错与成长的空间？ 这样，你不必再把所有重担都归因到「是我不够好」。

At Yunisle, we hold both your inner experience and your outer environments: Do they support your autonomy? Affirm your worth? Offer room to try, fail, and grow? This way, the weight doesn’t fall solely on “I’m not good enough.”

有时，你感到疲惫，不是因为你「太脆弱」，而是因为你长期在一个 缺乏支持、充满矛盾信息的系统里努力活着。 看到这一点，本身就是一种松一口气的开始。

Sometimes your exhaustion doesn’t mean you’re “too weak”, but that you’ve been trying to live in a system that offers little support and many conflicting signals. Seeing this clearly can already be a small exhale.

多流派对话：当不同心理学家的声音，在你身上相遇 Dialogues across traditions: Different voices meeting in your life

在寻屿，我们不会把你「塞进」某一个流派， 而是让不同理论在你的人生故事里展开对话： 既有精神分析对无意识与早期经验的凝视，也有人本与存在主义对自由与责任的关照， 还有动机理论、系统视角与叙事实践的加持。

We don’t force you into one school of thought. Instead, we let theories converse within your story: psychoanalytic depth on early experience, humanistic and existential attention to freedom and responsibility, alongside motivation theory, systemic thinking, and narrative practice.

从探索，到自我创造：我们一起走的一条路 From exploration to self-creation: A path we walk together

具体到与你的工作上，寻屿会把以上理论，编织成一条可以一步步走的路径： 探索 → 了解 → 接纳 → 承诺 → 行动 → 自我创造。

In practice, we weave these theories into a stepwise path we can walk together: Explore → Understand → Accept → Commit → Act → Self-create.

⟡ 探索： 先把你现在的处境、困惑与关系摊开来看。我们会用提问与绘图、时间线、小练习等方式， 让你看清：我正在经历什么？这些感受与反应，从何而来？

⟡ Explore: We begin by laying out your current situations, questions, and relationships. Through questions, mapping, timelines, and small exercises we ask: What am I going through? Where do these feelings and reactions come from?

⟡ 了解： 结合自我决定理论与生态系统视角，把「症状」翻译成「需求」与「历史」， 从「我怎么这么糟」转向「在这样的环境里，我是如何一路撑到现在」。

⟡ Understand: Using SDT and ecological theory, we translate “symptoms” into needs and history: shifting from “What’s wrong with me?” to “How have I survived this context so far?”

⟡ 接纳： 不是消极认命，而是承认：在那样的资源与约束下，我已经尽力了。 让羞耻与自责慢慢退场，为新的选择腾出空间。

⟡ Accept: Not resignation, but acknowledgement that given your resources and constraints, you really have tried. This softens shame and guilt, making room for new choices.

⟡ 承诺： 与其再许下「我要完全变成另一个人」，不如一起找到 一个可行、并与你的价值对齐的小承诺： 比如更诚实地面对一个关系、更温柔地对待一部分自己。

⟡ Commit: Instead of vowing to become “a totally different person”, we look for a small, realistic commitment that aligns with your values: perhaps a more honest conversation, or a kinder stance toward a part of yourself.

⟡ 行动： 把承诺落在具体的行为、节奏与边界上；我们会一起检查环境中的支持与阻力， 找到「下一步足够小，但足以带来新体验」的行动。

⟡ Act: We translate commitment into behaviour, pacing, and boundaries, checking for supports and barriers around you to find actions that are “small enough to take, big enough to feel”.

⟡ 自我创造： 长期来看，你会逐渐感到：自己不是被动地「被定义」，而是在现实与限制之中， 一点点塑造自己的生命叙事——这也是「寻屿」最在意的部分。

⟡ Self-create: Over time, you may feel less like someone merely “defined by others” and more like a person who, even within limits, is actively shaping their own life story—the heart of Yunisle’s work.
