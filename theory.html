Theory & Approach — 从理论到可走的路
自我决定理论 · Self-Determination Theory

我尤其关注自我决定理论中的三个核心心理需要：自主、胜任感与联结感。 当这些需要被看见、尊重和滋养时，人更有可能活得有内在动力、有方向，也更有力与他人建立真实的关系。

Self-Determination Theory

I work closely with Self-Determination Theory, especially the needs for autonomy, competence, and connectedness. When these needs are seen and supported, people are more likely to live with inner motivation, direction, and genuine connection.

探索 - 领悟 - 行动 的循环

在寻屿，我们常常沿着这样的节奏前行：

  • 探索：从经验出发，温柔而诚实地梳理发生了什么。
  • 领悟：理解背后的需要、信念与模式，找到“为什么”。
  • 行动：尝试新的选择，在具体情境中一点点调整与练习。
The cycle: Explore – Understand – Act

On Yunisle, we often move through a gentle cycle:

  • Explore: starting from lived experience, naming what has been happening.
  • Understand: seeing the needs, beliefs, and patterns beneath the surface.
  • Act: trying new responses and choices, grounded in real situations.

Note

I draw from humanistic, existential, systemic, and cognitive-behavioural traditions in an integrated way, guided by the person rather than by rigid schools.
