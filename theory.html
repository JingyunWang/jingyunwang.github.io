方法与路径 · Inner Compass

Inner Compass · The Framework

这不是一套“快速改变自己”的技巧，而是一份与你的生命史对齐的内在地图。

This is not a toolbox for “quick self-fixing”, but an inner map aligned with your own life story.

一条主线：自我决定理论
The Main Thread: Self-Determination Theory

我们以自我决定理论（Self-Determination Theory）为主线—— 人的心理健康与活力，建立在三个基本需要之上：

We ground our work in Self-Determination Theory: the idea that psychological health and vitality grow from three basic needs:

  • · 自主（Autonomy）
    · Autonomy

    不是“我一个人来”，而是“我感到这是出于我心的选择”。

    Not “I do everything alone,” but “this feels like my own chosen path.”

  • · 胜任（Competence）
    · Competence

    在挑战中感到自己有能力回应，而不是长期被无力感淹没。

    Feeling able to respond to challenges, instead of drowning in chronic helplessness.

  • · 连接（Relatedness）
    · Relatedness

    在关系中被看见、被理解，而不是永远扮演“懂事的人、没事的人”。

    Being seen and understood in relationships, rather than forever playing the “good, fine, low-need one”.

多条支线：融合多流派的温度
Side Threads: A Woven, Plural Approach

我接受过多种心理学取向训练，并在实践中融会贯通，而不是把来访者塞进某种“流派的框架”。

I’m trained in multiple therapeutic approaches and weave them together in practice, rather than forcing you into one rigid school of thought.

  • 人本主义 · 罗杰斯
    Humanistic · Carl Rogers

    “当一个人被深刻地倾听时，他会开始听见自己。”

    “When someone is deeply heard, they begin to hear themselves.”

  • 存在主义 · 维克多·弗兰克
    Existential · Viktor Frankl

    “人在任何境遇中仍然保有最后的自由——选择自己的态度。”

    “Everything can be taken from a person but one thing: the last of human freedoms—to choose one’s attitude.”

  • 依恋与关系 · 温尼科特
    Attachment & Relational · Winnicott

    “没有所谓‘单独的婴儿’，总有一个婴儿和一个照顾他的人。”

    “There is no such thing as a baby, only a baby and someone caring for them.”

一条路径：从经验 → 意识 → 行动 → 自我创造
One Path: Experience → Awareness → Action → Self-Creation
  • 1 · 经验：让生命的真实浮出水面
    1 · Experience: Let Reality Surface

    从你最近的生活片段、情绪波动、人际冲突开始——它们比抽象的大道理更诚实。

    We begin from concrete scenes: recent days, emotional spikes, relational tensions. They’re more honest than abstract “life lessons”.

  • 2 · 意识：看见模式与未被说出的需要
    2 · Awareness: See Patterns & Unspoken Needs

    在安全的对话中，我们一起识别：反复出现的模式背后，藏着怎样的恐惧、渴望与价值。

    In a safe dialogue, we trace repeating patterns and the fears, longings, and values that live behind them.

  • 3 · 行动：在现实边界内寻找新的可能
    3 · Action: Find New Possibilities Within Limits

    我们不会假装现实不存在，而是在现实的缝隙里，为你寻找可行的一小步。

    We don’t deny reality’s limits. We look for one doable, meaningful step inside them.

  • 4 · 自我创造：把“理解”变成新的故事
    4 · Self-Creation: Turn Insight into New Stories

    一次次选择之后，你会慢慢发现：你正在从“被期待的人”，变成“由自己创造的人”。

    Over time, you’ll notice: you’re moving from “the person others expected” to “a self you are actively creating”.

这套方法适合怎样的你？
Who Is This Approach For?
  • · 在高成就、高期待环境中长大，却常常觉得“我好像没有真正活过自己”的人。
    · Those raised in high-expectation, high-achievement contexts who often feel, “I’ve never really lived as myself.”
  • · 在亲密关系、家庭、工作选择之间摇摆，害怕辜负任何一方，包括自己的人。
    · Those torn between relationships, family, and career paths, afraid of disappointing everyone—including themselves.
  • · 已经读过很多心理学 / 自助书，却发现“懂了很多道理，还是过不好这一生”的人。
    · Those who have read many psychology books but still feel, “I understand a lot, but I still don’t know how to live better.”
