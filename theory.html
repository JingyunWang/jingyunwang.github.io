理论与路径：从自主到共创 Theory & Pathways: from autonomy to co-creation

我相信，好的心理工作不是“给答案”，而是帮助你发现，自己本来就有能力，选择与创造属于自己的答案。 这里是支撑这座“心灵岛屿”的理论与方法。

I believe that good psychological work does not hand you answers, but helps you discover that you can choose and create your own. Here are the theories and methods that hold this inner isle.

核心理论 · 自我决定理论（SDT） Core Theory · Self-Determination Theory (SDT)

人，天生有向着生命而去的力量

Humans are naturally oriented toward growth

自我决定理论认为，每个人都有三种基本心理需要：自主感、胜任感、联结感。
当这三者被支持时，人不会只停留在“生存”，而是会自发地迈向更真实、更有意义的生活。

Self-Determination Theory proposes three basic psychological needs: autonomy, competence, and relatedness.
When these needs are supported, we do not merely survive; we naturally move toward a more authentic and meaningful life.

自主感：我可以选择 Autonomy: I can choose 胜任感：我能做好 Competence: I can impact 联结感：我被看见 Relatedness: I am seen & held

在寻屿，每一次对话、每一次活动设计，都会围绕这三条主轴：
不是把你推向某种“正确的样子”，而是支持你，活成更像自己的样子。

At Yunisle, every conversation and every programme is designed around these needs—not to push you towards a “correct” self, but to support you in living a life more like you.

方法路径 · 探索-领悟-行动 Method · Exploration–Insight–Action

从经验出发，回到行动，再落在生活

From experience, to insight, to action, back to life

· 探索（Exploration）：允许一切如其所是，被看见。
· 领悟（Insight）：理解背后的模式、需要与价值。
· 行动（Action）：把领悟，变成可以落地的小步实践。
· 自我创造（Self-creation）：不断修订故事，成为更像自己的自己。

· Exploration: Let what is, be seen as it is.
· Insight: Understand the patterns, needs, and values underneath.
· Action: Turn insight into small, concrete steps.
· Self-creation: Keep rewriting your story, and become more yourself.

这条路径，会被编织进每一种形式：个体对话、心理团体、工作坊、阅读与写作练习……

This pathway is woven into every form: 1:1 conversations, groups, workshops, reading and writing practices, and more.
